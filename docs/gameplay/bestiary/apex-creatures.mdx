### Party Composition

For Apex creature farming, consider:

- **Tank** - Draw attention, manage positioning
- **Healer** - Sustained healing, cleanse support
- **DPS** - Prioritize burst over sustained damage

### Key Mechanics

1. **Death Explosions** - Many Apex creatures explode on death (Primordial Echo, Eclipse Horror)
2. **Healing Prevention** - Multiple Apex have anti-healing auras (Hollow One, Broken Seraph)
3. **CC Immunity** - Several gain CC immunity at low HP (Ember Revenant, Empty Warden)
4. **Pack Mechanics** - Legion Hound and similar require add management
