## Origin

Cassian was a **mortal man**-a charismatic army general who captured Alassa's heart. Their love was genuine, passionate, and doomed. When Alassa discovered his betrayal with a servant, her heartbreak became rage. She killed the servant and her family, then tore Cassian's heart from his chest-not killing him, but removing his capacity to feel anything.

Cassian wandered the world as a hollow shell-alive but empty, unable to feel love, hate, fear, or hope. When the Anguish Stone's corruption spread, it found his emptiness and filled it with power. He became Alassa's strongest general-bound to the final gate by whatever trace of their old bond still lingers.

<Callout type="info">
Some believe that if Cassian could be restored-if his heart could be returned-he might be turned against Alassa. This theory assumes he would want to turn, which assumes he can want anything at all.
</Callout>

## Strategy

### Recommended Party Composition

| Class | Count | Role |
|-------|-------|------|
| **Paladin** | 1-2 | Tank - Evasion builds, counter awareness |
| **Cleric** | 1-2 | Healer - Burst healing, timing coordination |
| **Warrior/Ranger/Mage** | 2-3 | DPS - Balanced damage |

### Key Tactics

1. **Avoid Shadow Slash** - Tanks position Cassian away from group
2. **Heal in Bursts** - Heart of Nothing reduces healing by 40%
3. **Dispel Empty Memory** - <SkillMention skill="purify" link={false}>Purify</SkillMention> stunned allies quickly
4. **Tank Cooldowns** - Perfect Strike is unblockable, use defensive cooldowns

### Common Mistakes

- Standing in Shadow Slash cone
- Trying to sustain through Heart of Nothing (use burst heals)
- Ignoring Empty Memory stuns (cleanse quickly)
- Tank not using defensives for Perfect Strike

## What Lies Beyond

Past Cassian stands the **Throne of Ruin**-Alassa's palace, the corrupted Eterna Citadel. There, bearing the full power of the Anguish Stone, she awaits those brave or foolish enough to challenge her.

<Callout type="warning" title="The Final Confrontation">
Defeating Cassian does not end the war. It only opens the door to the true battle. Alassa-connected to the Dark Titan's will, wielding the Anguish Stone's full power-awaits. The final war has just begun.
</Callout>
