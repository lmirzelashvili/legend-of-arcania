## The Path of Conquest

The Dominions must be conquered in sequence. Each General guards the gate to the next territory:

```
Zone 1: Vharun (Dreadmar Frontier)
    ↓
Zone 2: Saelistra (Mire of Whispers)
    ↓
Zone 3: Kharagul (Stonegrave Expanse)
    ↓
Zone 4: Morgathis (Eclipse Barrens)
    ↓
Zone 5: Cassian (Hall of Fallen)
    ↓
Final: Alassa (Throne of Ruin)
```

## Combat Classifications

All Generals are classified as **Dominion Boss (Raid)** encounters with the following characteristics:

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **HP** | 50x+ normal creatures |
| **Damage** | Raid-level, phase-dependent |
| **Party** | Full party required (4-6 players) |
| **AI** | Scripted phases with unique mechanics |
| **Rewards** | Legendary drops, achievement unlocks |
| **CC Immunity** | Immune to hard crowd control |

<Callout type="warning">
Dominion Boss encounters require coordinated parties of 4-6 players. Solo players should focus on clearing Area Bosses and regular creatures within each dominion before attempting the General.
</Callout>

## Area Bosses

Each dominion contains multiple **Area Bosses**-giant versions of regular creatures that guard key locations. These serve as progression checkpoints before reaching the General.

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **HP** | 15x normal creatures |
| **Damage** | 5x normal |
| **Party** | Small group (2-4 players) |
| **Rewards** | Unique area drops, map progression |

## General Profiles

### <img src="/game-icons/avatars/dominion_generals/vharun_avatar.png" alt="Vharun" className="inline w-10 h-10 mr-2 my-0 rounded-full align-middle" /> [Vharun the Fallen Warden](/docs/gameplay/bestiary/bosses/vharun)
**Zone 1: Dreadmar Frontier**

Once a guardian spirit who protected the land for ten thousand years. The Anguish Stone emptied his consciousness, leaving only instinct without purpose. He commands fire, stone, and volcanic energy.

- **Damage:** Physical
- **Defenses:** High Phys / Medium Magic
- **Special Mechanic:** Warden's Memory (brief vulnerability windows)

---

### <img src="/game-icons/avatars/dominion_generals/saelistra_avatar.png" alt="Saelistra" className="inline w-10 h-10 mr-2 my-0 rounded-full align-middle" /> [Saelistra the First Cry](/docs/gameplay/bestiary/bosses/saelistra)
**Zone 2: Mire of Whispers**

A Luminar healer of legendary compassion. The sheer volume of death during the war shattered her mind, and the Anguish Stone poured into the cracks. Now she weaponizes the grief of everyone who died in her care.

- **Damage:** Magic
- **Defenses:** Low Phys / Very High Magic
- **Special Mechanic:** The First Cry (traumatic memory assault)

---

### <img src="/game-icons/avatars/dominion_generals/kharagul_avatar.png" alt="Kharagul" className="inline w-10 h-10 mr-2 my-0 rounded-full align-middle" /> [Kharagul the Bloodthirster](/docs/gameplay/bestiary/bosses/kharagul)
**Zone 3: Stonegrave Expanse**

Not transformed-drawn forth. When the Anguish Stone's power reached Stonegrave, it awakened the rage and hunger buried in the Titans' remains. Kharagul is barely sentient-pure hunger given form.

- **Damage:** Physical
- **Defenses:** Very High Phys / Medium Magic
- **Special Mechanic:** Bloodthirst (grows stronger with each kill)

---

### <img src="/game-icons/avatars/dominion_generals/morgathis_avatar.png" alt="Morgathis" className="inline w-10 h-10 mr-2 my-0 rounded-full align-middle" /> [Morgathis, Hand of the Titan](/docs/gameplay/bestiary/bosses/morgathis)
**Zone 4: Eclipse Barrens**

An avatar of the Dark Titan's will-literally a colossal hand that emerged from a dimensional rift and gained autonomy. Morgathis embodies cosmic ambition and the drive to consume.

- **Damage:** Magic
- **Defenses:** Medium Phys / High Magic
- **Special Mechanic:** Titan's Whisper (tempts players to submit)

---

### <img src="/game-icons/avatars/dominion_generals/cassian_avatar.png" alt="Cassian" className="inline w-10 h-10 mr-2 my-0 rounded-full align-middle" /> [Cassian the Hollow](/docs/gameplay/bestiary/bosses/cassian)
**Zone 5: Hall of Fallen**

A mortal general who captured Alassa's heart, then betrayed her. She tore his heart from his chest-not killing him, but removing his capacity to feel. The Anguish Stone filled his emptiness with power.

- **Damage:** Physical
- **Defenses:** Very High Phys / High Magic
- **Special Mechanic:** The Empty Heart (immune to morale attacks, but can be reminded)

---

## Strategic Overview

### Difficulty Progression

| General | Challenge | Preparation |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Vharun | Survival | Magic damage dealers |
| Saelistra | Coordination | Physical damage dealers |
| Kharagul | Resource management | Magic damage dealers |
| Morgathis | Coordination | Physical damage dealers |
| Cassian | Everything | Best gear |

### Party Recommendations

<Callout type="tip">
While any balanced party can succeed, certain compositions excel in specific Dominions:

- **Dreadmar:** Magic damage dealers (Low Magic Res)
- **Mire:** Physical damage dealers (Low Phys Def)
- **Stonegrave:** Multiple tanks, magic damage
- **Eclipse:** Physical damage dealers (Med Phys Def)
- **Hall:** Balanced composition, high mobility
</Callout>

## The Final Gate

Defeating Cassian opens the path to the **Throne of Ruin**-Alassa's palace, the corrupted Eterna Citadel. There, bearing the full power of the Anguish Stone, she awaits the final confrontation.
