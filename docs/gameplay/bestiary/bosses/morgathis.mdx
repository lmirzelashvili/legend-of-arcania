## Origin

Morgathis was not born, transformed, or corrupted-it was **constructed**. When the Eterna Citadel fell, a colossal hand emerged from the dimensional rift and crushed it. That hand did not vanish. Instead, it detached from whatever larger form it belonged to and became Morgathis-an avatar of the Dark Titan's lingering will.

Morgathis is not truly alive. It is an **echo of cosmic ambition** given form-the Dark Titan's drive to consume and grow, concentrated into a being of incredible power. Some believe it has its own agenda: to serve as a seed from which the Dark Titan might one day return.

<Callout type="warning">
Morgathis's existence raises terrifying questions. If a hand can separate and become autonomous, what else might emerge? Is the Dark Titan truly dead, or merely scattered?
</Callout>

## Boss Abilities

| Ability | Type | Effect |
|---------|------|--------|
| **Eclipse Beam** | Line | 200 Magic damage + 30 DOT for 3s |
| **Eclipse Grasp** | Single | Pulls furthest target to melee range |

**Passive:**
- **Crushing Presence** - Aura: -15% damage dealt within 15m

## Strategy

### Recommended Party Composition

| Class | Count | Role |
|-------|-------|------|
| **Paladin** | 1 | Tank - Holy buffs reduce stun duration |
| **Cleric** | 1-2 | Healer - <SkillMention skill="purify" link={false}>Purify</SkillMention> essential, burst healing |
| **Warrior/Ranger** | 2-3 | DPS - Physical damage (exploits lower Magic Res) |

### Key Tactics

1. **Avoid Eclipse Beam** - Spread to minimize line damage
2. **Dispel Shadow Wave** - <SkillMention skill="purify" link={false}>Purify</SkillMention> removes the damage debuff
3. **Healers Stay Close** - Eclipse Grasp pulls furthest targets
4. **Account for Passive** - -15% damage dealt aura reduces overall DPS

### Common Mistakes

- Standing in Eclipse Beam path
- Letting Shadow Wave debuff stack on DPS players
- Healers standing far and getting pulled
- Not bringing physical damage dealers
