## Origin

Vharun was a **guardian spirit**-one of the First World entities that protected natural places before the gods walked the earth. For ten thousand years, he maintained what was then called the **Garden Frontier**, nurturing life and repelling threats.

When Alassa's forces arrived, Vharun stood against them alone. The Anguish Stone's power overwhelmed him-not destroying his consciousness, but **emptying it**. What remains is instinct without purpose, duty without meaning. He protects the Frontier still, but now "protection" means destroying all who enter.

<Callout type="info">
Unlike other generals who were created evil or chose corruption, Vharun was violated. Some believe that truly defeating him means not just killing his corrupted form, but freeing whatever remains of the guardian within.
</Callout>

## Strategy

### Recommended Party Composition

| Class | Count | Role |
|-------|-------|------|
| **Paladin** | 1 | Tank - Taunt rotation, <SkillMention skill="shield_bond" link={false}>Shield Bond</SkillMention> |
| **Cleric** | 1-2 | Healer - Sustained healing, <SkillMention skill="purify" link={false}>Purify</SkillMention> |
| **Mage** | 2-3 | DPS - Magic damage (exploits low Magic Res) |

### Key Tactics

1. **Spread for Volcanic Slam** - Avoid stacking to minimize AoE damage
2. **Dispel Magma Chains** - Clerics should <SkillMention skill="purify" link={false}>Purify</SkillMention> rooted allies quickly
3. **Move During Eruption** - Find safe zones when room-wide damage activates
4. **Sustained Healing** - Passive burn damage requires constant healing pressure

### Common Mistakes

- Stacking up and taking full Volcanic Slam damage
- Leaving allies rooted in Magma Chains
- Bringing only Physical damage dealers
- Neglecting fire resistance
