## Combat Tips

### Elite Creature Patterns

Elite creatures follow predictable engagement patterns:

1. **Enhanced abilities** - All base creature abilities with increased damage/duration
2. **New mechanics** - At least one additional ability not found in Hostile versions
3. **Resource management** - Longer fights requiring mana/cooldown awareness

### Recommended Strategies

| Zone | Strategy |
|------|----------|
| Homelands | Learn patterns, practice interrupts |
| Convergence | Coordinate with party, cleanse debuffs |
| Breach | Full party recommended, watch for summons |
| Dreadmar | Bring fire resistance, interrupt Molten Sentinel's Shell |
