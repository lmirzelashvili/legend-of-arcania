## The Nature of Corruption

When the Void Titan and Dark Titan destroyed each other in **The Anguish**, their clash released energy that still permeates reality. The <AnguishStoneMention>Anguish Stone</AnguishStoneMention>-a crystallized fragment of that primordial rage-allows Alassa to channel this power, creating and corrupting creatures of immense strength.

<Callout type="warning">
Every creature in the Dominion exists because of the <AnguishStoneMention>Anguish Stone</AnguishStoneMention>. Destroying Alassa would sever their connection to this power-but until then, they are bound to her will.
</Callout>

## Combat Mechanics

Dominion creatures deal either **Physical** or **Magic** damage:

| Damage Type | Mitigated By | Creature Examples |
|-------------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Physical** | <DerivedStatMention stat="physical_defense" link={false}>Physical Defense</DerivedStatMention> | Melee brutes, beasts, soldiers |
| **Magic** | <DerivedStatMention stat="magic_resistance" link={false}>Magic Resistance</DerivedStatMention> | Casters, spirits, manifestations |

<Callout type="info">
Build your defenses accordingly: stack <DerivedStatMention stat="physical_defense" link={false}>**Physical Defense**</DerivedStatMention> (VIT) against brutes and melee creatures, or <DerivedStatMention stat="magic_resistance" link={false}>**Magic Resistance**</DerivedStatMention> (SPR) against casters and spirits.
</Callout>

## Combat Strategies

### General Principles

1. **Know Your Enemy** - Check if creatures deal Physical or Magic damage
2. **Build Defenses** - Stack <DerivedStatMention stat="physical_defense" link={false}>Physical Defense</DerivedStatMention> or <DerivedStatMention stat="magic_resistance" link={false}>Magic Resistance</DerivedStatMention> as needed
3. **Target Low Defenses** - Most magic creatures have low <DerivedStatMention stat="physical_defense" link={false}>Physical Defense</DerivedStatMention>
4. **Manage Resources** - Longer fights in deeper zones; conserve mana

### Class Advantages

| Class | Best Zone | Reason |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| <ClassMention class="warrior">**Warrior**</ClassMention> | Stonegrave | High Physical Defense, sustained damage |
| <ClassMention class="paladin">**Paladin**</ClassMention> | Eclipse | Balanced defenses, group support |
| <ClassMention class="mage">**Mage**</ClassMention> | Dreadmar | High Magic damage vs low Magic Res |
| <ClassMention class="cleric">**Cleric**</ClassMention> | Mire | Healing sustain, Magic Resistance |
| <ClassMention class="ranger">**Ranger**</ClassMention> | Hall | Mobility, Physical damage at range |

## The Path Forward

To reach Alassa at the Throne of Ruin, all five Dominions must be conquered in sequence. Each General must fall before the next territory opens.

```
Valoryn → Dreadmar → Mire → Stonegrave → Eclipse → Hall → Throne of Ruin
```
