## Primary Stats (5)

Primary stats are the five core attributes you directly allocate points to. Each affects multiple derived stats.

<Callout type="info">
Primary stats come **only** from level-up point allocation and racial bonuses. Equipment provides derived stats (like <DerivedStatMention stat="attack_speed" link={false}>Attack Speed</DerivedStatMention> or <DerivedStatMention stat="max_hp" link={false}>Max HP</DerivedStatMention>) but not primary stats like STR or VIT.
</Callout>

## Derived Stats (13)

Derived stats are calculated from primary stats, equipment bonuses, buffs, and other sources. Equipment provides derived stats through slot identity bonuses and socketed gems.

### Stat Categories

```
Primary Stats (5)
    │
    ├── Offensive (7)
    │   ├── Physical Attack
    │   ├── Magic Attack
    │   ├── Critical Chance
    │   ├── Critical Damage
    │   ├── Attack Speed
    │   ├── Armor Penetration
    │   └── Magic Penetration
    │
    ├── Defensive (2)
    │   ├── Physical Defense
    │   └── Magic Resistance
    │
    ├── Resources (4)
    │   ├── Max HP
    │   ├── Max Mana
    │   ├── HP Regeneration
    │   └── Mana Regeneration
    │
    └── Utility (1)
        └── Movement Speed
```

### How Stats Affect Abilities

Abilities scale from **derived stats**, not primary stats directly:

```
Level Up → Allocate Primary Stats (STR, INT, etc.)
    ↓
Primary Stats convert to Derived Stats
    ↓
Equipment adds more Derived Stats
    ↓
Abilities scale from total Derived Stats
```

| Class | Abilities Scale From |
|-------|---------------------|
| <ClassMention class="warrior" link={false}>Warrior</ClassMention> | <DerivedStatMention stat="physical_attack" link={false}>Physical Attack</DerivedStatMention> |
| <ClassMention class="paladin" link={false}>Paladin</ClassMention> | <DerivedStatMention stat="physical_attack" link={false}>Physical Attack</DerivedStatMention> (+ <DerivedStatMention stat="magic_attack" link={false}>Magic Attack</DerivedStatMention> for hybrid) |
| <ClassMention class="ranger" link={false}>Ranger</ClassMention> | <DerivedStatMention stat="physical_attack" link={false}>Physical Attack</DerivedStatMention> |
| <ClassMention class="mage" link={false}>Mage</ClassMention> | <DerivedStatMention stat="magic_attack" link={false}>Magic Attack</DerivedStatMention> |
| <ClassMention class="cleric" link={false}>Cleric</ClassMention> | <DerivedStatMention stat="magic_attack" link={false}>Magic Attack</DerivedStatMention> |

<Callout type="info">
This means equipment directly affects ability damage. A sword with +50 <DerivedStatMention stat="physical_attack" link={false}>Physical Attack</DerivedStatMention> adds +50 damage (×skill multiplier) to Warrior abilities!
</Callout>

### <DerivedStatMention stat="magic_penetration" link={false}>Magic Penetration</DerivedStatMention>

<FormulaBlock title="Magic Penetration">

Reduces enemy Magic Resistance by the percentage before damage calculation.

## Defensive Stats

### <DerivedStatMention stat="physical_defense" link={false}>Physical Defense</DerivedStatMention>

<FormulaBlock title="Physical Defense">
Physical Defense = (VIT × 2) + Armor Value + Equipment Stats
Damage Reduction = Defense / (Defense + 100 + AttackerLevel × 5)
</FormulaBlock>

| Defense | vs Level 50 | Reduction |
|---------|-------------|-----------|
| 200 | 200 / 550 | 36% |
| 400 | 400 / 750 | 53% |
| 600 | 600 / 950 | 63% |
| 800 | 800 / 1150 | 70% |

### <DerivedStatMention stat="magic_resistance" link={false}>Magic Resistance</DerivedStatMention>

<FormulaBlock title="Magic Resistance">
Magic Resistance = (SPR × 2) + Magic Armor + Equipment Stats
Magic Reduction = Resistance / (Resistance + 100 + AttackerLevel × 5)
</FormulaBlock>

## Example Calculations

### Level 50 Warrior

**Stats:** STR 150, INT 20, VIT 80, AGI 100, SPR 20

<FormulaBlock title="Calculated Stats">
Physical Attack = 150 × 2 = 300 (+ weapon)
Physical Defense = 80 × 2 = 160 (+ armor)
Max HP = 150 + (80 × 12) + (50 × 15) = 1,860
Critical Chance = 5% + (100 × 0.12%) = 17%
Critical Damage = 125% + (100 × 0.25%) = 150%
Attack Speed = 1.0 × (1 + 100 × 0.005) = 1.5 APS
Armor Penetration = 150 × 0.05% = 7.5%
</FormulaBlock>

### Level 50 Mage

**Stats:** STR 20, INT 150, VIT 60, AGI 80, SPR 100

<FormulaBlock title="Calculated Stats">
Magic Attack = 150 × 2.5 = 375 (+ weapon)
Magic Resistance = 100 × 2 = 200 (+ armor)
Max HP = 100 + (60 × 12) + (50 × 15) = 1,570
Max Mana = 200 + (150 × 8) + (100 × 4) + (50 × 8) = 2,200
Critical Chance = 5% + (80 × 0.12%) = 14.6%
Magic Penetration = 150 × 0.05% = 7.5%
</FormulaBlock>

## Character Deletion & Recovery

<Callout type="warning">
Characters above level 10 have a 7-day recovery period. Delete carefully!
</Callout>

### Deletion Rules
- Characters below level 10: Immediate deletion
- Characters level 10+: 7-day recovery period

### Recovery
- Deleted characters can be recovered within 7 days
- All items and progress preserved
- Gold has 10% tax on recovery
