## The Ranger Tradition

> "The trees remember when there were no gods. The rivers recall when the only light was starlight. I do not command the wild-I listen. And the wild whispers secrets older than divinity itself."
> - Grove Warden Sylana, First Listener of Lythora

### Origin of the First Listeners

Before Anias awakened, she dreamed. For ages beyond counting, while she slumbered in the void after the Titans' destruction, her unconscious mind radiated creative energy. This energy took form: forests grew, rivers flowed, creatures emerged-all born from the peaceful dreams of a sleeping goddess.

This was the **First World**-creation without intention, life without divine plan. And within it, the Lilin emerged as Anias's dream-daughters, the first beings to exist.

When Anias finally awakened and began consciously shaping reality, the First World didn't disappear. It simply... receded. It became the wild places, the ancient forests, the untamed corners where primal energy still pulses stronger than divine order.

The first Rangers were those who learned to hear the **Earthsong**-the voice of the First World, still echoing through creation. They didn't discover a new power. They remembered an old one-older than the gods themselves.

### Philosophy of the Wild

Rangers do not worship the First World or pray to it. They commune with it. Their relationship with primal power is fundamentally different from divine classes: they listen rather than petition, harmonize rather than command.

### Notable Figures

#### Grove Warden Sylana
First Listener of Lythora and founder of Ranger training in the First Forest. A Lilin who has communed with the Earthsong for longer than most civilizations have existed. She teaches that the First World is not a tool to be used, but a family member to be honored.

#### The First Listeners
The legendary Rangers who first learned to hear the Earthsong. Unlike other orders, the First Listeners weren't founded by a single figure-they emerged naturally, in different places and times, as mortals learned to listen to the wild. The title "First Listener" is now given to master Rangers who can hear the deepest whispers of the First World.

#### Warden-Commander Thorne
Current leader of Ranger forces in the Grand Alliance. A human who spent twenty years in Lythora learning from the Lilin before returning to lead his people's scouts and skirmishers. He bridges the gap between the practical military needs of the war and the spiritual traditions of Ranger training.

#### The Dreamwalkers
A specialized order of Luminar Rangers who overcame their race's natural difficulty with the First World. By harmonizing their conscious divine light with the unconscious Earthsong, they became something new-bridges between Anias's waking and sleeping creations. They are rare, but their insights have deepened understanding of both divine and primal power.

## Playstyle

Rangers are **mobile skirmishers** who control the battlefield from a distance. They excel at **kiting** (staying mobile while dealing damage) and **utility** (traps, slows, vision).

### Combat Flow

```
Position at Range → Apply Traps → Build Focus with Aimed Shots → Control Distance → Devastate with Arrow Rain
```

Rangers never want enemies close. Their entire kit revolves around **creating and maintaining distance**.
