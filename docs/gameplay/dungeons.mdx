## Unlocking Dungeons

Dungeons unlock through quest progression:

| Dungeon | Unlock Requirement |
|---------|-------------------|
| Ashen Depths | Complete "Frontier Scout" quest (Level 30) |
| Grief Hollow | Complete "Into the Mire" quest (Level 45) |
| Titan's Maw | Complete "Bones of the Fallen" quest (Level 60) |
| Void Sanctum | Complete "Shadow's Edge" quest (Level 65) |
| Hollow Citadel | Complete "The Empty Throne" quest (Level 75) |

## Dungeon Tips

- **Party bonus:** +10% rewards per additional player (up to +40%)
- **Bring consumables:** Potions and elixirs are essential for later dungeons
- **Watch mechanics:** Mini-bosses teach mechanics needed for dungeon bosses
- **Manage cooldowns:** Plan your daily entries around peak play times
