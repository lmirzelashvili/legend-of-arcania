---
title: Accessories
description: Pendants, rings, wings, and capes with hybrid random stats for all classes.
---

Accessories are supplementary equipment worn by all classes. They use the **hybrid random stat system** with an identity stat and random bonus stats, but have **NO sockets**. This keeps accessory progression distinct from armor/weapon progression.

---

## Accessory Stat Model

Each accessory provides:

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Identity Stat** | Guaranteed stat type for this accessory |
| **Prestige Stat** | Unique Prestige bonus (only on Prestige quality drops) |
| **Random Bonus Stats** | 1-3 stats rolled from a themed pool |

<Callout type="info">
**No Sockets:** Unlike armor, weapons, and shields, accessories cannot be socketed with gems. Their power comes entirely from identity, Prestige, and bonus stats.
</Callout>

## Prestige Accessories

Accessories have a 10% chance to drop as **Prestige** quality. Each type gains a unique Prestige stat:

| Accessory | Prestige Stat | T1 | T2 | T3 | T4 | T5 |
|-----------|---------------|----|----|----|----|-----|
| **Pendant** | Prestige Crit % | +0.4 | +0.8 | +1.2 | +2.0 | +3.5 |
| **Ring** | Prestige Atk Spd % | +0.4 | +0.8 | +1.2 | +2.0 | +3.5 |
| **Wings** | Prestige Phys Def | +4 | +10 | +18 | +30 | +50 |
| **Cape** | Prestige Magic Atk | +5 | +12 | +22 | +36 | +60 |

<Callout type="info">
Prestige accessories are marked with a golden border. The Prestige stat stacks with all other bonuses.
</Callout>

## Set Bonuses

Wearing matching Pendant and Rings of the same tier grants Crit Damage % bonuses.

| Set | 2-Piece Bonus | 3-Piece Bonus |
|-----|---------------|---------------|
| **Vharun** | +2% Crit Damage | +4% Crit Damage |
| **Saelistra** | +4% Crit Damage | +8% Crit Damage |
| **Kharagul** | +6% Crit Damage | +12% Crit Damage |
| **Morgathis** | +8% Crit Damage | +16% Crit Damage |
| **Cassian** | +12% Crit Damage | +24% Crit Damage |

### Set Composition

- **2-piece**: 1 Pendant + 1 Ring
- **3-piece**: 1 Pendant + 2 Rings (maximum)

<Callout type="tip">
Wings and Capes are NOT part of sets - they follow different naming conventions and are class-restricted. Mix and match your Pendant + Rings for set bonuses while using Wings/Capes for additional stats.
</Callout>

## Example: Tier 5 Pendant of Cassian

**Standard God Roll:**
```
Pendant of Cassian (Tier 5)
├── Identity: Crit Chance +6% (max)
├── Bonus 1: Max HP +360 (max)
├── Bonus 2: Crit Damage +15% (max)
└── Bonus 3: Physical Attack +50 (max)
```

**Prestige God Roll:**
```
★ Pendant of Cassian (Tier 5) [PRESTIGE]
├── Identity: Crit Chance +6% (max)
├── Prestige: Prestige Crit +3.5%
├── Bonus 1: Max HP +360 (max)
├── Bonus 2: Crit Damage +15% (max)
└── Bonus 3: Physical Attack +50 (max)
```
