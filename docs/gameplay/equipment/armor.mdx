---
title: Armor
description: Class-specific armor sets with hybrid random stats for engaging loot farming.
---

Armor in Arcania is class-specific. Each piece uses the **hybrid random stat system** with an identity stat, random bonus stats, and sockets for gems.

---

## Equipment Slots

All armor sets cover five body slots. Each slot has a guaranteed identity stat and draws bonus stats from a themed pool:

| Slot | Identity Stat | Prestige Stat | Bonus Pool Theme |
|------|---------------|---------------|------------------|
| **Head** | <DerivedStatMention stat="mana_regen" link={false}>Mana Regeneration</DerivedStatMention> | Prestige Crit % | Magic |
| **Chest** | <DerivedStatMention stat="max_hp" link={false}>Max HP</DerivedStatMention> | Prestige HP Regen | Survivability |
| **Gloves** | <DerivedStatMention stat="attack_speed" link={false}>Attack Speed %</DerivedStatMention> | Prestige Crit Dmg % | Offensive |
| **Legs** | <DerivedStatMention stat="hp_regen" link={false}>HP Regeneration</DerivedStatMention> | Prestige Move % | Endurance |
| **Boots** | <DerivedStatMention stat="movement_speed" link={false}>Movement Speed %</DerivedStatMention> | Prestige Atk Spd % | Mobility |

<Callout type="info">
Equipment provides derived stats only - not primary stats like STR or VIT. Primary stats come from level-up point allocation.
</Callout>

## Prestige Armor

Armor has a 10% chance to drop as **Prestige** quality. Each slot gains a unique Prestige stat:

| Slot | Prestige Stat | T1 | T2 | T3 | T4 | T5 |
|------|---------------|----|----|----|----|-----|
| **Head** | Prestige Crit % | +0.5 | +1.0 | +1.5 | +2.5 | +4.0 |
| **Chest** | Prestige HP Regen | +0.3 | +0.6 | +1.0 | +1.6 | +2.5 |
| **Gloves** | Prestige Crit Dmg % | +1 | +2 | +4 | +7 | +12 |
| **Legs** | Prestige Move % | +1 | +2 | +3 | +5 | +8 |
| **Boots** | Prestige Atk Spd % | +0.5 | +1.0 | +2.0 | +3.5 | +6.0 |

<Callout type="info">
A full Prestige Tier 5 armor set provides significant stat bonuses on top of standard stats. Prestige items are visually marked with a golden border.
</Callout>
