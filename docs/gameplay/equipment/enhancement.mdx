---
title: Enhancement
description: Upgrade equipment with crystals to increase stats up to +15.
---

Enhancement allows you to upgrade any equipment piece to increase its base stats. Different crystals are required for different enhancement levels.

---

## How Enhancement Works

1. **Select equipment** to enhance
2. **Use the correct crystal** for your target level
3. **Attempt enhancement** - success or failure based on rates
4. **Repeat** up to +15 maximum

<Callout type="info">
Enhancement applies to all equipment: weapons, armor, shields, and accessories.
</Callout>

---

## Crystal Requirements

| Level Range | Required Crystal |
|-------------|------------------|
| +1 to +7 | <CrystalMention crystal="spirit">Crystal of Spirit</CrystalMention> |
| +8 to +15 | <CrystalMention crystal="dominion">Crystal of Dominion</CrystalMention> |

<Callout type="warning">
You MUST use the correct crystal type. Crystal of Spirit cannot enhance beyond +7. Crystal of Dominion is required for +8 and above.
</Callout>

---

## Crystal of Spirit

*Used for early enhancement stages (+1 to +7)*

- **Level Range:** +1 to +7 only
- **Acquisition:** World drops, vendors, quests
- **Cost:** Common, easily farmable

### Spirit Success Rates

| Target Level | Success Rate | On Failure |
|--------------|--------------|------------|
| +1 | 95% | Crystal consumed |
| +2 | 90% | Crystal consumed |
| +3 | 85% | Crystal consumed |
| +4 | 75% | Crystal consumed |
| +5 | 65% | Drops to +4 |
| +6 | 55% | Drops to +4 |
| +7 | 45% | Drops to +4 |

---

## Crystal of Dominion

*Required for advanced enhancement stages (+8 to +15)*

- **Level Range:** +8 to +15 only
- **Acquisition:** Elite enemies, Dominion bosses, rare vendors
- **Cost:** Rare, valuable resource

### Dominion Success Rates

| Target Level | Success Rate | On Failure |
|--------------|--------------|------------|
| +8 | 40% | Drops to +5 |
| +9 | 35% | Drops to +5 |
| +10 | 30% | Drops to +5 |
| +11 | 25% | **Item DESTROYED** |
| +12 | 20% | **Item DESTROYED** |
| +13 | 15% | **Item DESTROYED** |
| +14 | 10% | **Item DESTROYED** |
| +15 | 5% | **Item DESTROYED** |

<Callout type="error">
Enhancement from +11 onwards risks permanent item destruction. There is no protection - failed attempts destroy the item completely.
</Callout>

## Risk Tiers

| Level | Risk Tier | On Failure |
|-------|-----------|------------|
| +1 to +4 | Safe | Crystal consumed |
| +5 to +7 | Risky | Drops to +4 |
| +8 to +10 | Dangerous | Drops to +5 |
| +11 to +15 | Extreme | Item DESTROYED |

## Enhancement Strategies

### Conservative Build (+4 Safe)
- Crystal of Spirit to +4
- No level drop on failure - only crystal consumed
- Safest approach for valuable items

### Standard Build (+7 to +10)
- Crystal of Spirit to +7 (failures drop to +4)
- Crystal of Dominion to +10 (failures drop to +5)
- Items always survive, just need patience and crystals

### Maximum Power (+15)
- Full enhancement path
- Prepare backup equipment
- Only enhance farmable/replaceable items beyond +10
- Keep valuable items at +10 for safety

## Example: +15 Weapon

```
Chaos Emperor Greatsword +15
├── Base Damage: 180-220
├── Enhancement Bonus: +50%
├── Final Damage: 270-330
└── Status: Maximum Enhancement
```

<Callout type="tip">
A +15 weapon deals 50% more damage than an unenhanced version. Combined with good random stats and gems, this creates massive power differences.
</Callout>
