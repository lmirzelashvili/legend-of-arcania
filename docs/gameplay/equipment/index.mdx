Equipment follows a **five-tier progression system**. Higher tiers provide increased stats. More items will be added to each tier over time.

---

## Prestige System

All equipment has a chance to drop as **Prestige** quality. Prestige items have an additional **Prestige Identity Stat** unique to each equipment slot.

| Quality | Drop Rate | Bonus |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| **Standard** | 90% | Normal stats only |
| **Prestige** | 10% | +Prestige Identity Stat |

### Prestige Identity Stats by Slot

Each slot gains a specific Prestige bonus when dropped as Prestige quality:

| Slot | Prestige Stat | T1 | T2 | T3 | T4 | T5 |
|------|---------------|----|----|----|----|-----|
| **Weapon** | Prestige Damage | +5 | +10 | +18 | +28 | +45 |
| **Head** | Prestige Crit % | +0.5 | +1.0 | +1.5 | +2.5 | +4.0 |
| **Chest** | Prestige HP Regen | +0.3 | +0.6 | +1.0 | +1.6 | +2.5 |
| **Gloves** | Prestige Crit Dmg % | +1 | +2 | +4 | +7 | +12 |
| **Legs** | Prestige Move % | +1 | +2 | +3 | +5 | +8 |
| **Boots** | Prestige Atk Spd % | +0.5 | +1.0 | +2.0 | +3.5 | +6.0 |
| **Shield** | Prestige Block % | +1 | +2 | +3 | +4 | +6 |
| **Pendant** | Prestige Crit % | +0.4 | +0.8 | +1.2 | +2.0 | +3.5 |
| **Ring** | Prestige Atk Spd % | +0.4 | +0.8 | +1.2 | +2.0 | +3.5 |
| **Wings** | Prestige Phys Def | +4 | +10 | +18 | +30 | +50 |
| **Cape** | Prestige Magic Atk | +5 | +12 | +22 | +36 | +60 |

<Callout type="info">
Prestige items are visually distinct with a golden border. The Prestige stat is fixed per slot - you cannot reroll which Prestige stat you get.
</Callout>

<Callout type="tip">
Use a <MaterialMention material="hand_of_forgemaster">Hand of Forgemaster</MaterialMention> (very rare) to transform any Standard item into Prestige quality.
</Callout>

## Tier System & Level Requirements

Equipment follows a **five-tier progression system** with level requirements. You must reach the required level to equip items of each tier.

| Tier | Req. Level | Power Level |
|------|------------|-------------|
| Tier 1 | 1 | Starting equipment |
| Tier 2 | 11 | Improved stats |
| Tier 3 | 26 | Specialized gear |
| Tier 4 | 41 | High-tier equipment |
| Tier 5 | 56 | Endgame gear |

<Callout type="info">
**Level Requirements:** All equipment pieces (weapons, armor, shields, accessories) require a minimum character level to equip. Attempting to equip gear above your level will fail.
</Callout>
