Participate in scheduled events for exclusive rewards. All events unlock at **Level 10** and run **8 times daily** (every 3 hours) to cover all timezones.

---

## Events Overview

| Event | Duration | Players | Focus |
|-------|----------|---------|-------|
| **Ember Run** | 1 hour | 1-5 | Items & Gear |
| **Dusk Tower** | 1 hour | 1-5 | EXP & Gold |
| **Dominion Assault** | 1 hour | 1-5 | Materials & Gems |

<Callout type="info">
Solo players receive full rewards. Party play grants **+10% bonus rewards per additional player** (up to +40% with 5 players).
</Callout>

---

## Event Levels

Each event has **5 difficulty levels**. Choose your level when entering.

### Level Requirements

| Event Level | Recommended Level | Monster Level | Difficulty |
|-------------|-------------------|---------------|------------|
| **Level 1** | 10-20 | ~15 | Easy |
| **Level 2** | 21-35 | ~28 | Normal |
| **Level 3** | 36-50 | ~43 | Hard |
| **Level 4** | 51-70 | ~60 | Very Hard |
| **Level 5** | 71-85 | ~78 | Extreme |

<Callout type="tip">
**Free Choice:** You can attempt any event level regardless of your character level. Higher levels offer significantly better rewards but tougher monsters.
</Callout>

## Ember Run

Speed clear focused on **equipment drops**. Race through gates and defeat the boss.

### How It Works

1. Select difficulty level (1-5)
2. Enter the run (solo or party)
3. Kill monsters to open gates
4. Clear 3 gates
5. Defeat the final boss

## Dusk Tower

Floor climb focused on **massive EXP and Gold gains**. Clear floors to maximize rewards.

### How It Works

1. Select difficulty level (1-5)
2. Enter the tower (solo or party)
3. Clear all monsters on each floor
4. Advance through 10 floors
5. Defeat the boss on floor 10

### Rewards by Level (Full Clear)

| Level | Total XP | Total Gold | Magic Box |
|-------|----------|------------|-----------|
| 1 | 3,000 | <img src="/game-icons/gold.png" alt="Gold" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 500 | Box of Ash |

**Floor Distribution:**

## Dominion Assault

Wave survival focused on **materials and gems**. Farm resources from endless waves.

### How It Works

1. Select difficulty level (1-5)
2. Enter the arena (solo or party)
3. Survive waves of monsters
4. Boss spawns every 5 waves
5. Survive as many waves as possible (max 20)

### Rewards by Level (Wave 20 Clear)

| Level | Crystals | Gems | XP |
|-------|----------|------|-----|
| 1 | Crystal of Spirit x5 | - | 500 |
| 2 | Crystal of Spirit x10 | Gem of Void | 1,500 |
| 3 | Crystal of Dominion x5 | Gem of Void | 4,000 |
| 4 | Crystal of Dominion x10 | Gem of Light | 8,000 |
| 5 | Crystal of Creation x5 | Gem of Anguish | 15,000 |

**Wave Milestones:**
- Wave 5: 20% of total rewards
- Wave 10: 40% of total rewards + first gem
- Wave 15: 70% of total rewards
- Wave 20: 100% of total rewards + bonus gem

<Callout type="info">
Higher event levels have stronger monster waves but the same wave structure. The challenge is surviving with tougher enemies.
</Callout>

## Tips

- **Low-level players:** Start with Level 1 events to learn mechanics and get gear appropriate to your level
- **High-level players:** Level 5 events offer the best rewards in the game - don't skip them!
- **Farming efficiently:** Match event level to your gear, not just your character level
- **Party bonus:** The +40% party bonus applies to all event levels - group up for maximum rewards
