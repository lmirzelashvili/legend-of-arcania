The **Forgemaster Anvil** is Arcania's central crafting station where players forge powerful equipment from gathered materials.

---

## Forgemaster Anvil

The Forgemaster Anvil allows you to combine materials into equipment. Unlike drops, forged items let you work toward specific goals - but success is never guaranteed.

### How Forging Works

1. **Select a recipe** from the Anvil menu
2. **Add required materials** (primary material + Crystal of Creation)
3. **Optional:** Add Crystal of Spirit or Dominion to boost success rate
4. **Attempt forging** - success creates the item, failure consumes materials

<Callout type="warning">
Forging is risky. Higher tier items have lower success rates. Add crystals to improve your odds.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Prestige Quality:** All successfully forged items are always Prestige quality.
</Callout>

## Success Rates

Base success rates vary by tier. Add crystals to improve your chances - **more crystals = higher bonus**:

| Tier | Base Rate |
|------|-----------|
| T1 | 60% |
| T2 | 40% |
| T3 | 25% |
| T4 | 12% |
| T5 | 5% |

### Crystal Bonuses (Stackable)

| Crystal | Bonus Per Crystal | Max Quantity | Max Bonus |
|---------|-------------------|--------------|-----------|
| <CrystalMention crystal="spirit">**Crystal of Spirit**</CrystalMention> | +0.5% per crystal | 30 | +15% |
| <CrystalMention crystal="dominion">**Crystal of Dominion**</CrystalMention> | +1% per crystal | 20 | +20% |

**Combined Maximum:** +35% (30 Spirit + 20 Dominion)

### Example: Tier 5 Forging

| Crystals Used | Success Rate |
|---------------|--------------|
| None | 5% |
| 10x Spirit | 10% |
| 30x Spirit | 20% |
| 20x Dominion | 25% |
| 30x Spirit + 20x Dominion | 40% |

<Callout type="tip">
For Tier 5 forging, use maximum crystals (30 Spirit + 20 Dominion) to reach 40% success rate.
</Callout>

## Failure Mechanics

When forging fails, materials are handled differently:

| Material | On Failure |
|----------|------------|
| <CrystalMention crystal="creation">**Crystal of Creation**</CrystalMention> | Always consumed |
| <CrystalMention crystal="spirit">**Crystal of Spirit**</CrystalMention> | Always consumed |
| <CrystalMention crystal="dominion">**Crystal of Dominion**</CrystalMention> | Always consumed |
| <MaterialMention material="feather_of_roc" link={false}>**Feathers**</MaterialMention> / <MaterialMention material="thread_of_silkworm" link={false}>**Thread of Silkworm**</MaterialMention> | 20-70% returned (random) |

<Callout type="info">
Primary materials (Feathers, Thread of Silkworm) have a random chance to be returned on failure. You might recover most of them, or lose everything - it's random each attempt.
</Callout>

### <MaterialMention material="finger_of_titan" link={false}>Fingers of Titan</MaterialMention>
*Forge random T4-T5 armor from existing sets*

Combine <MaterialMention material="finger_of_titan" link={false}>Fingers of Titan</MaterialMention> to forge a random armor piece from the game's existing armor sets.

| Material | Amount | Crystal of Creation |
|----------|--------|---------------------|
| <MaterialMention material="finger_of_titan" link={false}>Finger of Titan</MaterialMention> | 250 | 3 |

**What's Random:**
- **Tier:** T4 or T5 (50/50 chance)
- **Slot:** Any armor slot (Head, Chest, Gloves, Legs, Boots)
- **Set:** Any class armor set (Divine Bastion, Dark Berserker, Ancient Venom, Manticore Regalia, etc.)
- **Stats:** Normal random stat rolls for that piece

**Success Rate:** 40% base (use crystals to boost up to 70%)

<Callout type="info">
The forged armor follows the same stat and set bonus rules as dropped armor. You might get exactly what you need, or something to trade with other players.
</Callout>

## Forging Tips

- **Start low:** Practice on Tier 1-2 items to learn the system
- **Stock materials:** Farm extra Feathers/Silk before attempting high tiers
- **Always boost T5:** The 5% base rate is brutal - use both crystal types
