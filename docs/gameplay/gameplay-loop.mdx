The core loop of Arcania follows a progression through increasingly challenging content, better gear, and higher stakes. Along the way, daily activities, events, and social features keep gameplay fresh.

---

## Core Loop

```
CREATE CHARACTER
       │
       ▼
┌─────────────────┐
│   HOMELAND      │  Learn basics, reach level 10
│   (Safe Zone)   │
└────────┬────────┘
         │
         ▼
┌─────────────────┐
│   FREE REALMS   │  Level 10-50, quests, gear up
│   (Safe Zones)  │
└────────┬────────┘
         │
         ▼

```

## Phase 1: Homeland (Level 1-10)

Your starting zone based on race. Safe environment to learn mechanics.

| Activity | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Tutorial quests | Learn controls, combat, UI |
| Kill monsters | Gain XP, collect loot |
| Visit vendors | Buy potions, basic gear |
| Unlock abilities | Learn class skills |

**Goal:** Reach level 10, complete homeland quests, enter Valoryn.

---

## Phase 2: Free Realms (Level 10-50)

Explore the five Free Realms. Safe zones with no item drop on death.

| Activity | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Zone quests | XP, Gold, story progression |
| Grind monsters | Level up, farm materials |
| Unlock Outposts | Fast travel, respawn points |
| Upgrade gear | T1 → T2 → T3 equipment |
| Join parties | Tackle harder content |
| **Events** (Level 10+) | Bonus XP, gear, materials (5 difficulty levels) |
| **Dungeons** (Level 30+) | Instanced challenges, exclusive rewards |

**Goal:** Reach level 50, gear up for Dominions.

---

## Phase 3: Dominions (Level 50+)

Danger Zones with full-loot PvP and the game's hardest content.

| Activity | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Dominion quests | Best XP and rewards |
| Hunt elite/apex creatures | Rare drops, materials |
| Challenge Dominion Bosses | T4-T5 gear, achievements |
| PvP encounters | Risk vs reward gameplay |
| Manage risk | Store valuables in Vault |
| **Higher-tier Dungeons** | T4-T5 gear, rare materials |
| **Level 5 Events** | Best rewards from highest difficulty |

**Goal:** Defeat bosses, acquire T4-T5 gear, climb rankings.

---

## Phase 4: Endgame

Max level content focused on optimization and competition.

| Activity | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Farm Dominion Bosses | Best-in-slot gear |
| Enhance equipment | +1 to +15 upgrades |
| Marketplace trading | Build wealth |
| PvP rankings | Compete for leaderboards |
| Battle Pass progression | Seasonal rewards |
| Achievement hunting | Complete all milestones |
| Arcanite Exchange | Convert to ARC Token |

## Daily Activities

Content that resets daily or runs on schedules.

### Login Streaks

Log in daily to build your streak (resets if you miss a day):

| Day | Reward |
|-----|--------|
| 1-2 | <img src="/game-icons/gold.png" alt="Gold" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 1,000-2,000 Gold |
| 3 | <img src="/game-icons/arcanite.png" alt="Arcanite" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 5 Arcanite |
| 4-5 | <img src="/game-icons/gold.png" alt="Gold" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 5,000 Gold + Box of Cinder |
| 6 | <img src="/game-icons/arcanite.png" alt="Arcanite" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 10 Arcanite |
| 7 | <img src="/game-icons/gold.png" alt="Gold" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 25,000 Gold + Mega Booster (8h) |

After Day 7, you keep receiving Day 7 rewards. Missing a day resets to Day 1.

### Dungeons

Instanced PvE challenges with daily entry limits:

| Dungeon | Level | Daily Entries | Focus |
|---------|-------|---------------|-------|
| Ashen Depths | 30-40 | 3 | T3 gear, Crystals |
| Grief Hollow | 45-55 | 3 | T3-T4 gear, Gems |
| Titan's Maw | 60-70 | 2 | T4 gear, Gems |
| Void Sanctum | 65-75 | 2 | T4-T5 gear, rare Gems |
| Hollow Citadel | 75-85 | 2 | T5 gear, Hand of Forgemaster |

<Callout type="info">
Party bonus: **+10% rewards per player** (up to +40% with 5 players).
</Callout>

### Timed Events

Three events rotate every hour, running **8 times daily each** (every 3 hours). Each event has 5 difficulty levels:

| Event | Unlocks | Focus | Level 5 Rewards |
|-------|---------|-------|-----------------|
| **Ember Run** | Level 10 | Equipment | 15,000 XP, T5 gear, Box of Eclipse |
| **Dusk Tower** | Level 10 | XP & Gold | 100,000 XP + <img src="/game-icons/gold.png" alt="Gold" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 25,000 Gold |
| **Dominion Assault** | Level 10 | Materials | Crystal of Creation, Gem of Anguish |

Check the [Events](/docs/gameplay/events) page for all difficulty levels and rewards.

## Social Features

### Party System

Group up with 2-5 players for bonus rewards:

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **+10% rewards per player** | Stacks up to +40% with 5 players |
| **Shared XP** | All party members gain XP from kills |
| **Dungeon access** | Required for harder content |
| **Event bonuses** | Same party bonus applies |

### Social Events

Chat-based community events with rewards:

| Event | How to Win | Rewards |
|-------|------------|---------|
| **Chat Drops** | Be active in chat, click to claim | Gold, items, Arcanite |
| **Trivia** | Answer questions first | Gold, Arcanite |
| **Word Scramble** | Unscramble names first | Gold, Arcanite |

## Seasonal Content

### Battle Pass

90-day seasons with 80 tiers of rewards:

| Track | Cost | Highlights |
|-------|------|------------|
| **Free** | Free | Gold, consumables, basic cosmetics |
| **Premium** | $2.99 | <img src="/game-icons/arcanite.png" alt="Arcanite" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 750 Arcanite total, exclusive cosmetics, boosters |

Progress by earning XP from any activity. Premium unlocks retroactively.

### PvP Seasons

3-month competitive seasons with rankings:

| Rank | Season Rewards |
|------|----------------|
| Top 10 | Exclusive title, cosmetic set, <img src="/game-icons/arcanite.png" alt="Arcanite" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 1,000 Arcanite |
| Top 100 | Seasonal title, cosmetic, <img src="/game-icons/arcanite.png" alt="Arcanite" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 500 Arcanite |
| Top 1,000 | Seasonal title, <img src="/game-icons/arcanite.png" alt="Arcanite" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 200 Arcanite |
| Top 10% | Seasonal badge, <img src="/game-icons/arcanite.png" alt="Arcanite" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 100 Arcanite |

## Progression Pillars

| Pillar | How You Progress |
|--------|------------------|
| **Level** | XP from kills, quests, events, dungeons |
| **Gear** | Drops, dungeons, Marketplace, enhancement |
| **Wealth** | Gold from gameplay, Arcanite from achievements |
| **Reputation** | PvP rankings, achievement completion |
| **Knowledge** | Learning zones, boss mechanics, event strategies |
