The war against the Dominion cannot be won alone. Arcania's party system allows adventurers to band together, combining their strengths to tackle challenges that would overwhelm any solo fighter.

---

## Party Size & Experience Bonuses

Larger parties earn bonus experience on all kills. This bonus applies to each party member individually.

| Party Size | XP Bonus | Total Party XP |
|------------|----------|----------------|
| Solo | 0% | 100% |
| 2 members | +10% | 220% (110% each) |
| 3 members | +20% | 360% (120% each) |
| 4 members | +30% | 520% (130% each) |
| 5 members | +40% | 700% (140% each) |

<Callout type="tip">
A full party of 5 members earns 40% more XP per person than solo players. Group up whenever possible for faster leveling.
</Callout>

---

## Level Restrictions

Party members must be within **10 levels** of each other to receive experience from kills.

| Scenario | Result |
|----------|--------|
| Level 20 + Level 25 | Both receive XP (5 level difference) |
| Level 20 + Level 30 | Both receive XP (10 level difference) |
| Level 20 + Level 31 | Level 31 receives no XP (11 level difference) |

<Callout type="warning">
If you're outside the level range, you won't receive experience even if you contribute to the kill. The party bonus still applies to eligible members.
</Callout>

## Loot Distribution

Arcania uses a **free-for-all** loot system. When enemies drop items, any party member can pick them up.

### How It Works

- Drops appear on the ground for all party members to see
- First player to pick up an item claims it
- No automatic distribution or rolling system
- Party members should communicate about valuable drops

<Callout type="info">
Coordinate with your party on loot priorities before entering difficult content. Many groups designate drops based on class or need.
</Callout>

## Party Roles

Effective parties balance three core roles:

### Tank
Draws enemy attention and absorbs damage.

- **Primary:** <ClassMention class="paladin">[Paladin](/docs/gameplay/classes/paladin)</ClassMention>
- **Secondary:** <ClassMention class="warrior">[Warrior](/docs/gameplay/classes/warrior)</ClassMention> (with defensive build)

### Healer
Keeps the party alive through sustained healing.

- **Primary:** <ClassMention class="cleric">[Cleric](/docs/gameplay/classes/cleric)</ClassMention>

### Damage Dealer (DPS)
Eliminates enemies as quickly as possible.

- **Ranged:** <ClassMention class="mage">[Mage](/docs/gameplay/classes/mage)</ClassMention>, <ClassMention class="ranger">[Ranger](/docs/gameplay/classes/ranger)</ClassMention>
- **Melee:** <ClassMention class="warrior">[Warrior](/docs/gameplay/classes/warrior)</ClassMention>
