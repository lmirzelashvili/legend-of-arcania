> "Each Dominion is ruled by a General. Each General guards the gate to the next. Defeat them all, or die trying."
> - War Council Briefing

The Dominion Campaign is the heart of Arcania's quest system. Five zones, five Generals, 75 quests. Each dominion must be conquered in sequence to reach the Throne of Ruin.

---

## Campaign Overview

| Dominion | Levels | Quests | General | Theme |
|----------|--------|--------|---------|-------|
| <TerritoryMention territory="dreadmar" link={false}>**Dreadmar Frontier**</TerritoryMention> | 35-40 | 15 | Vharun | Fire, ash, volcanic corruption |
| <TerritoryMention territory="mire" link={false}>**Mire of Whispers**</TerritoryMention> | 40-45 | 15 | Saelistra | Swamp, fog, grief, madness |
| <TerritoryMention territory="stonegrave" link={false}>**Stonegrave Expanse**</TerritoryMention> | 45-50 | 15 | Kharagul | Titan bones, hunger, wasteland |

| <TerritoryMention territory="hall" link={false}>**Hall of Fallen**</TerritoryMention> | 53-55 | 15 | Cassian | Ruined citadel, emptiness |

### Progression Requirements

Each dominion unlocks the next via achievement:
- **Frontier Reclamation** → Unlocks Mire of Whispers
- **Mire Navigation** → Unlocks Stonegrave Expanse
- **Expanse Survivor** → Unlocks Eclipse Barrens
- **Barrens Survivor** → Unlocks Hall of Fallen
- **Hall Conqueror** → Unlocks Throne of Ruin

### Story Summary

Dreadmar Frontier was once the Garden Frontier - a verdant borderland protected by Vharun for ten thousand years. Now it's a realm of ash and flame, and Vharun himself has become its executioner.

The campaign teaches you how the Anguish Stone corrupts. Vharun wasn't created evil - he was *transformed*. His brief moments of clarity during the boss fight, where he remembers what he once protected, are heartbreaking.

**Ember's Rest** becomes your mid-dominion sanctuary. Commander Forge establishes the camp in cooled lava caves where volcanic heat is bearable - a rare pocket of safety in the inferno.

> "I remember green. I remember protecting. Now there is only fire. Why do you make me kill you?"
> - <img src="/game-icons/avatars/dominion_generals/vharun_avatar.png" alt="" className="inline w-5 h-5 mr-1 my-0 rounded-full align-middle" /> Vharun, during Memory phase

### Story Summary

The Hall of Fallen is the shattered remains of the Eterna Citadel - where the gods once gathered, where Anias died, where Alassa's grief began. Cassian guards the final gate with nothing left to lose.

**Aldric the Undying** leads this campaign personally. He knew Cassian before the fall. Their dialogue reveals the tragedy: Cassian was once a hero, Alassa's champion and lover. When grief consumed Alassa, it hollowed Cassian too - not corrupting him, but *emptying* him.

**Ghostspeaker Moira** at The Last Hearth can commune with echoes of the past. Through her, you learn what Cassian was before - and understand what defeating him truly means.

> "I feel nothing. I remember feeling. Is there a difference? I cannot tell anymore."
> - <img src="/game-icons/avatars/dominion_generals/cassian_avatar.png" alt="" className="inline w-5 h-5 mr-1 my-0 rounded-full align-middle" /> Cassian the Hollow

Aldric has optional dialogue throughout this campaign. Speaking with him reveals the history between Cassian and the First Paladin - and adds emotional weight to the final confrontation.
</Callout>
