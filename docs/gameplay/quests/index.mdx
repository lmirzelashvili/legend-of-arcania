The quest system in Arcania follows a structured progression from your racial homeland through to the final confrontation with Alassa. All quests serve the war effort against the Dominion.

---

## Quest Progression Overview

```
HOMELAND → CONVERGENCE → VALORYN → THE BREACH → DOMINIONS → THRONE OF RUIN
```

## Quest Chain Categories

### By Phase

- [**Homeland Quests**](/docs/gameplay/quests/homeland-quests) - 4 unique racial chains (Meridia, Lythora, Redmire, Lumeria)
- [**Valoryn Quests**](/docs/gameplay/quests/valoryn-quests) - Convergence, Training, and Class milestone quests
- [**Dominion Quests**](/docs/gameplay/quests/dominion-quests) - All 5 dominion campaign chains + The Breach
- [**Throne of Ruin**](/docs/gameplay/quests/throne-quests) - The final campaign against Alassa

## Characters Who Return

Several NPCs guide you across multiple phases of your journey:

<Callout type="info" title="Recurring Characters">
- **Elder Thessaly** - Your guide in Lythora returns to lead Mire of Whispers operations
- **Lightkeeper Seris** - Your mentor in Lumeria becomes a key figure at Dawnkeeper Outpost
- **Aldric the Undying** - The First Paladin who trains you in Valoryn personally leads the Hall of Fallen campaign
</Callout>

## Boss Progression

Each phase culminates in increasingly challenging boss encounters:

| Phase | Boss | Type |
|-------|------|------|
| Homeland | Area Bosses (4 per path) | Solo/Duo |
| Convergence | The Fallen Guardian | Group skill check |
| Breach | The Gatekeeper | Party required |
| Dreadmar | Vharun the Fallen Warden | Dominion General |
| Mire | Saelistra the First Cry | Dominion General |
| Stonegrave | Kharagul the Bloodthirster | Dominion General |
| Eclipse | Morgathis, Hand of the Titan | Dominion General |
| Hall | Cassian the Hollow | Dominion General |
| Throne | Alassa, the Dread Emissary | Final Boss |

<Callout type="tip">
Defeating each Dominion General unlocks an achievement that grants access to the next dominion. You must complete each zone in sequence.
</Callout>
