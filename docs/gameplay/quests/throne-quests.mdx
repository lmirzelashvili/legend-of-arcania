## Quest Details

### Quest 1: The Final March

*"Every leader. Every soldier. Every prayer. We gather one last time."*

The War Council convenes at Valoryn's Sunward Keep. Every faction leader has words for you:

| Leader | Dialogue Theme |
|--------|----------------|
| **Erion** | The weight of being the last hope |
| **Kira Ironhand** | Tactical assessment of Alassa's forces |
| **Lilith** | Intelligence on the Anguish Stone |
| **Thorin Stonefist** | Ensures your equipment is ready |
| **Aurelia** | Blessing and prayer |
| **Aldric** | Personal encouragement, mentions Cassian |
| **Marael** | Reveals Alassa was not always evil |

**Lore Unlock: Alassa's Story**

You learn the full truth about Alassa - how she was once a healer, devoted to Anias. How she fell in love with Cassian. How watching Nimue murder her goddess broke something fundamental in her soul. The Anguish Stone didn't corrupt Alassa; it gave form to grief that already consumed her.

### Quest 2-3: Anguish Corridor

The approach to the Throne is guarded by empowered versions of creatures from all five Dominions. The Anguish Stone draws power from the grief of the entire world, and that power manifests here.

**Empowered Creatures:**
- Enhanced Cinder Hounds (Dreadmar)
- Amplified Grief Specters (Mire)
- Ravenous Bone Constructs (Stonegrave)
- Void-touched Eclipse Walkers (Eclipse)
- Hollow Knights (Hall)

The variety tests everything you've learned across the Dominion campaign.

---

### Quest 4: Anguish Fragment

**Elite Boss: Anguish Fragment**

A piece of the Anguish Stone itself, given sentient form. This is not a creature corrupted by the Stone - it IS the Stone's will made manifest.

| Phase | Mechanic |
|-------|----------|
| **Phase 1** | Cycles through elements of all five Dominions |
| **Phase 2** | Psychic attacks that show your character's fears |
| **Phase 3** | Reality distortion, platforms shift and change |

**Reward:** <MaterialMention material="hand_of_forgemaster" link={false}>Hand of Forgemaster</MaterialMention> x1 - One of the rarest items in the game, capable of transforming standard equipment to Prestige quality.

### Quest 5-6: Chamber of Grief

The Chamber of Grief is where Alassa's pain is most concentrated. Here, you face **Titan's Herald** - the most powerful Apex creature in the game.

**Apex Boss: Titan's Herald**

A herald announcing the Dark Titan's return, empowered by both Alassa's grief and remnant Titan energy.

| Detail | Information |

| **Type** | Apex (highest non-boss tier) |

This weapon is the best in-slot item for your class. Defeating the Herald proves you're ready for Alassa herself.

Environmental details reveal the history: scorch marks from divine conflict, crystallized tears, the shattered remains of what was once beautiful.

### Quest 8: Alassa's Sanctum

**Point of No Return**

Entering the Sanctum begins the final sequence. There is no retreat, no resupply, no rest.

The Sanctum is a twisted reflection of divine beauty - crystal spires corrupted into obsidian daggers, light that hurts rather than heals, love transformed into chains.

At the center waits Alassa.

## Multiple Endings

Your choice in Phase 4 determines your ending:

### Ending 1: Destruction

*"Some wounds cannot heal. Some grief is too deep. End it."*

You destroy Alassa and shatter the Anguish Stone. The Dominion collapses. Victory is absolute but hollow - another god is dead, and the one who loved her most is gone.

**Requirements:** Default ending if no special conditions met
**Reward:** Title "Destroyer of Grief"

### Ending 2: Redemption

*"She was a healer once. Perhaps she can be again."*

When given the choice at the end of Phase 2, you choose to attempt purification. The Anguish Stone is separated from Alassa, and the healer within is freed - broken, grieving, but no longer consumed.

**Reward:** Title "Redeemer of the Fallen"

### Ending 3: The Titan's Return

*"You have failed. The darkness rises again."*

If the party wipes during the final phase, the Dark Titan's essence reforms through the Anguish Stone. This is the "bad" ending.

**Trigger:** Party wipe during Phase 2
**Result:** Retry boss fight
