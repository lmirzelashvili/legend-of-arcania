Consumables provide instant restoration or temporary benefits during combat and exploration.

---

## Elixirs

### <ElixirMention elixir="life" size="lg" link={false}>Elixir of Life</ElixirMention>

Restores HP instantly. Higher sizes restore more health.

| Size | HP Restored | Cooldown |
|------|-------------|----------|
| <ElixirMention elixir="life" size="sm" link={false}>**SM**</ElixirMention> | 150 HP | 10s |
| <ElixirMention elixir="life" size="md" link={false}>**MD**</ElixirMention> | 350 HP | 10s |
| <ElixirMention elixir="life" size="lg" link={false}>**LG**</ElixirMention> | 600 HP | 10s |
| <ElixirMention elixir="life" size="xl" link={false}>**XL**</ElixirMention> | 1000 HP | 10s |

### <ElixirMention elixir="power" size="lg" link={false}>Elixir of Power</ElixirMention>

Temporarily increases offensive stats. Higher sizes provide stronger buffs for longer durations.

| Size | Effect | Duration |
|------|--------|----------|
| <ElixirMention elixir="power" size="sm" link={false}>**SM**</ElixirMention> | +5% Physical Attack, +5% Magic Attack | 5 min |
| <ElixirMention elixir="power" size="md" link={false}>**MD**</ElixirMention> | +10% Physical Attack, +10% Magic Attack | 10 min |
| <ElixirMention elixir="power" size="lg" link={false}>**LG**</ElixirMention> | +15% Physical Attack, +15% Magic Attack | 15 min |
| <ElixirMention elixir="power" size="xl" link={false}>**XL**</ElixirMention> | +20% Physical Attack, +20% Magic Attack | 30 min |

<Callout type="info">
Elixir of Power buff does not stack with itself. Using a new elixir replaces the current buff.
</Callout>
