Crystals are essential resources used throughout Arcania for enhancement, forging, and item creation.

---

## Crystal Types

| Crystal | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| <CrystalMention crystal="spirit">**Crystal of Spirit**</CrystalMention> | Enhancement (+1 to +7), Forging boost (+10%) |
| <CrystalMention crystal="dominion">**Crystal of Dominion**</CrystalMention> | Enhancement (+8 to +15), Forging boost (+20%) |
| <CrystalMention crystal="creation">**Crystal of Creation**</CrystalMention> | Required for all Forging recipes |

<Callout type="warning">
You MUST use the correct crystal type. Crystal of Spirit cannot enhance beyond +7. Crystal of Dominion is required for +8 and above.
</Callout>

---

## Crystal of Spirit

*Used for early enhancement stages (+1 to +7)*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Level Range** | +1 to +7 only |
| **Rarity** | Common |
| **Tradeable** | Yes |

### Acquisition Sources

| Source | Drop Rate |
|--------|-----------|
| World enemies | Common |
| Vendors | Purchasable (daily limit) |
| Quests | Reward |
| Dominion zones | Frequent |

### Enhancement Rates

| Target | Success | On Failure |
|--------|---------|------------|
| +1 | 95% | Crystal consumed |
| +2 | 90% | Crystal consumed |
| +3 | 85% | Crystal consumed |
| +4 | 75% | Crystal consumed |
| +5 | 65% | Drops to +4 |
| +6 | 55% | Drops to +4 |
| +7 | 45% | Drops to +4 |

---

## Crystal of Dominion

*Required for advanced enhancement stages (+8 to +15)*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Level Range** | +8 to +15 only |
| **Rarity** | Rare |
| **Tradeable** | Yes |

### Acquisition Sources

| Source | Drop Rate |
|--------|-----------|
| Elite enemies | Uncommon |
| Dominion bosses | Guaranteed |
| Rare vendors | Limited stock |
| Legendary quests | Reward |

### Enhancement Rates

| Target | Success | On Failure |
|--------|---------|------------|
| +8 | 40% | Drops to +5 |
| +9 | 35% | Drops to +5 |
| +10 | 30% | Drops to +5 |
| +11 | 25% | **DESTROYED** |
| +12 | 20% | **DESTROYED** |
| +13 | 15% | **DESTROYED** |
| +14 | 10% | **DESTROYED** |
| +15 | 5% | **DESTROYED** |

<Callout type="error">
Enhancement from +11 onwards risks permanent item destruction. There is no protection - failed attempts destroy the item completely.
</Callout>

## Stat Bonuses from Enhancement

| Level Range | Bonus Per Level | Cumulative |
|-------------|-----------------|------------|
| +1 to +5 | +2% base stats | +10% at +5 |
| +6 to +10 | +3% base stats | +25% at +10 |
| +11 to +15 | +5% base stats | +50% at +15 |

<Callout type="tip">
See [Enhancement](/docs/gameplay/equipment/enhancement) for full enhancement strategies and examples.
</Callout>

## Crystal of Creation

*Required for all forging recipes at the Forgemaster Anvil.*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Rarity** | Common |
| **Tradeable** | Yes |
| **Use** | Forging at Forgemaster Anvil |

### Acquisition Sources

| Source | Drop Rate |
|--------|-----------|
| Vendors | Purchasable |
| Dominion zones | Common |
| World enemies | Common |

### Usage in Forging

| Tier | Crystals Required |
|------|-------------------|
| T1 | 1 |
| T2 | 2 |
| T3 | 3 |
| T4 | 4 |
| T5 | 6 |

<Callout type="warning">
Crystal of Creation is always consumed on forging attempts, whether successful or not.
</Callout>

<Callout type="tip">
See [Forging](/docs/gameplay/forging) for complete recipes and success rates.
</Callout>
