Resources are items consumed during gameplay for crafting, enhancement, utility, and combat support.

---

## Resource Categories

| Category | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **Magic Boxes** | Tiered loot boxes containing equipment, gold, and materials |
| **Crystals** | Enhancement materials for upgrading equipment |
| **Gems** | Socketable items that add stats to equipment |
| **Scrolls** | Utility items for teleportation, protection, and item modification |
| **Consumables** | Potions, food, and temporary buffs |
| **Materials** | Crafting ingredients and trade goods |
