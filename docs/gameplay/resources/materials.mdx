### <MaterialMention material="finger_of_titan" link={false}>Finger of Titan</MaterialMention>

*Collectible fragments used to forge random T4-T5 armor.*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Rarity** | Uncommon |
| **Tradeable** | Yes |
| **Use** | Forge random armor at Forgemaster Anvil |

Combine 250 Fingers of Titan at the Forgemaster Anvil to create a random T4 or T5 armor piece. The tier, slot, class, and stats are all randomized.

**Acquisition Sources:**

| Source | Drop Rate |
|--------|-----------|
| Dominion Generals | Guaranteed (5-10) |
| Dominion bosses | Guaranteed (10-20) |
| Dominion zone enemies | Rare |

<Callout type="tip">
Farm Dominion Generals regularly to accumulate Fingers of Titan. At 250 per attempt, you'll need patience!
</Callout>

## Tools

### <MaterialMention material="hand_of_blacksmith" link={false}>Hand of Blacksmith</MaterialMention>

*Removes socketed gems from equipment, destroying the gem but preserving the item.*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Rarity** | Common |
| **Tradeable** | Yes |
| **Use** | Remove gem from socket |

Once a gem is socketed, it cannot be removed normally. The Hand of Blacksmith is the only way to remove a socketed gem. The gem is destroyed in the process, but the equipment remains intact with an empty socket ready for a new gem.

**Acquisition Sources:**

| Source | Drop Rate |
|--------|-----------|
| Vendors | Purchasable |
| World enemies | Common |
| Dominion zones | Common |

<Callout type="info">
Use Hand of Blacksmith when you want to replace a bad gem roll with a better one. The socket becomes empty and available for a new gem.
</Callout>

---

### <MaterialMention material="hand_of_forgemaster" link={false}>Hand of Forgemaster</MaterialMention>

*Transforms a Standard quality item into Prestige quality.*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Rarity** | Very Rare |
| **Tradeable** | Yes |
| **Use** | Upgrade Standard → Prestige |

The Hand of Forgemaster is one of the rarest items in Arcania. It adds the Prestige Identity Stat to any Standard quality equipment piece, effectively transforming it into a Prestige drop.

**Acquisition Sources:**

| Source | Drop Rate |
|--------|-----------|
| Dominion Generals | Very Rare |
| Dominion boss events | Very Rare |

<Callout type="warning">
The Hand of Forgemaster is consumed on use. Use it only on equipment you plan to keep long-term - ideally Tier 5 items with excellent random stat rolls.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
The Prestige stat added matches the slot's Prestige Identity Stat at maximum value for that tier. A Tier 5 weapon gains +45 Prestige Damage, a Tier 5 Chest gains +2.5 Prestige HP Regen, etc.
</Callout>
