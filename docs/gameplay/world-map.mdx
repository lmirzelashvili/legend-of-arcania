## World Progression

```
HOMELAND → CONVERGENCE → VALORYN → THE BREACH → DOMINIONS → THRONE OF RUIN
```

---

## Phase 1: Homeland Paths

Each race begins their journey in their homeland and travels through unique regions before all paths converge near Valoryn.

### Meridia Path (Humans)

The human path leads from the fertile farmlands through increasingly dangerous frontier territory.

| Area | Sub-Areas | Theme |
|------|-----------|-------|
| **Golden Fields** | I - VI | Last peaceful farmlands, shadows creeping |
| **Pilgrim's Road** | I - VI | Ancient trade routes, now haunted |
| **Briarwood Depths** | I - VIII | Dark forest swallowing the land |
| **Watchfire Ridge** | I - VIII | Crumbling outposts, desperate defenders |

## Phase 4: The Five Dominions

The Dominions must be conquered in sequence. Each is ruled by a Dominion General who guards the gateway to the next territory. Defeat the boss to advance.

## Phase 5: Throne of Ruin

*The Final Zone*

Beyond Cassian lies the Throne of Ruin - the corrupted heart of what was once the Eterna Citadel. Here, Alassa awaits with the full power of the Anguish Stone.

| Area | Sub-Areas | Final Battle |
|------|-----------|--------------|
| **Anguish Corridor** | I - XIV | |
| **Chamber of Grief** | I - XIV | |
| **The Broken Throne** | I - XIV | |
| **Alassa's Sanctum** | — | → **Alassa, the Dread Emissary** |

<img src="/game-icons/creatures icons/hostile_creatures_icon.png" alt="" className="inline w-5 h-5 mr-1 my-0 align-middle" /> **Creatures:** Elite and Apex creatures from all 5 Dominions, empowered by the Anguish Stone

<Callout type="warning" title="The Final Confrontation">
The battle against Alassa is the culmination of everything. She wields the Anguish Stone's full power and commands the grief of two centuries. This is not merely a fight - it is the war's final chapter.
</Callout>
