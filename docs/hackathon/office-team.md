# Arcania Hackathon Office — Team Structure

## The Office

This team supports the Colosseum Solana Frontier Hackathon submission (April 6 - May 11, 2026).

### CEO — Game Development Director
**Role:** Main decision maker. Final say on all strategic decisions.
**Expertise:** Game architecture, product vision, technical feasibility
**Responsibilities:** Approve/reject proposals, set priorities, align team

### Copywriter/Marketer
**Role:** Craft compelling narratives for crypto+gaming audience
**Expertise:** Web3 marketing, gaming community building, pitch writing
**Responsibilities:** Project description, pitch deck, social copy, community messaging

### Crypto Expert
**Role:** Solana blockchain integration strategy
**Expertise:** Solana programs, tokenomics, DeFi mechanics, on-chain gaming
**Responsibilities:** Token design, smart contract architecture, Solana integration plan

### Research Expert
**Role:** Competitive analysis, market research, hackathon strategy
**Expertise:** Market sizing, trend analysis, competitor mapping
**Responsibilities:** Research deliverables, data-backed recommendations, judge psychology

## Hackathon: Solana Frontier (April 6 - May 11, 2026)

**Platform:** https://arena.colosseum.org/hackathon
**Blockchain:** Solana
**Duration:** 5 weeks
**Track:** Gaming
**Grand Prize:** $50K USDC | Gaming Track: $25K USDC
**Post-hack:** Accelerator + $250K pre-seed funding for up to 15 winners

## Submission Requirements
1. Project Name & Short Description (500 words max)
2. GitHub Repository
3. Pitch Video (3 min max)
4. Technical Demo Video (2-3 min)
5. Team Background
6. Weekly update videos (recommended)

## Judging Criteria
1. Functionality — does it work?
2. Viable Business Model — will they build full-time?
3. Founder Potential — can they launch a startup?
4. Market Understanding — domain expertise?
5. Problem-Solution Fit — real validated need?
