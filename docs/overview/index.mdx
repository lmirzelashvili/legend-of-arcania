**Arcania** is a browser-based 2D MMORPG that combines nostalgic pixel-art aesthetics with modern web technologies to deliver an accessible yet deep MMO experience.

## The War for Arcania

Two hundred years ago, tragedy shattered the divine family. Anias, the goddess of creation, was murdered. Her husband Marael, god of order, transformed their daughter Alassa into the Dread Emissary - an instrument of vengeance wielding the <AnguishStoneMention>Anguish Stone</AnguishStoneMention>, a crystallized fragment of primordial destruction.

Now Erion, the last of the gods, leads the Grand Alliance against his corrupted sister. Four races have united at Valoryn, the heart of resistance, to push back against the Dominion's nightmare armies.

## Your Journey

The heart of Arcania follows a progression through an world at war:

1. **Choose Your Path** - Select from 4 races and 5 classes, each with unique lore and abilities
2. **Homeland Quests** - Begin your journey in your race's homeland
3. **Rally at Valoryn** - Join the Grand Alliance at the central fortress
4. **Venture into the Dominions** - Enter contested territories ruled by corrupted generals
5. **Face the Five Generals** - Challenge the Dominion Bosses who guard each territory
6. **Approach the Throne of Ruin** - The ultimate confrontation awaits
