## Daily Spin

Spin the wheel daily for a chance to win Creation Tokens and in-game currency. The spinner uses a CS:GO-style reel animation.

### How It Works

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to the **Daily Spin** section
3. Use your **3 free spins** per day
4. Collect your rewards

### Spin Rewards

| Reward | Chance |
|--------|--------|
| <img src="/game-icons/gold.png" alt="Gold" className="inline w-5 h-5 align-middle" /> 100 Gold | 45% |
| <img src="/game-icons/gold.png" alt="Gold" className="inline w-5 h-5 align-middle" /> 300 Gold + <img src="/game-icons/arcanite.png" alt="Arcanite" className="inline w-5 h-5 align-middle" /> 2 Arcanite | 30% |
| <img src="/game-icons/gold.png" alt="Gold" className="inline w-5 h-5 align-middle" /> 500 Gold + <img src="/game-icons/arcanite.png" alt="Arcanite" className="inline w-5 h-5 align-middle" /> 3 Arcanite | 15% |
| <img src="/game-icons/creation_token.png" alt="Creation Token" className="inline w-5 h-5 align-middle" /> **Creation Token** | 10% |

<Callout type="tip">
With 3 spins per day at 10% each, you have roughly a 27% chance to win a Creation Token daily. On average, most players get a token within 4-5 days of spinning.
</Callout>

### Spin Rules

- **3 free spins per day** - resets at 00:00 server time
- All spins have identical odds
- No streak bonuses
- Rewards are instant

## Account Wallet

Before creating a character, your rewards are stored in your **Account Wallet**.

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| <img src="/game-icons/gold.png" alt="Gold" className="inline w-5 h-5 align-middle" /> **Gold** | Accumulated from Daily Spins |
| <img src="/game-icons/arcanite.png" alt="Arcanite" className="inline w-5 h-5 align-middle" /> **Arcanite** | Accumulated from Daily Spins |
| <img src="/game-icons/creation_token.png" alt="Creation Token" className="inline w-5 h-5 align-middle" /> **Creation Tokens** | Used to create characters |

### What Happens After Character Creation?

When you create your first character:
- All Gold transfers to your character
- All Arcanite transfers to your Vault
- 1 Creation Token is consumed

<Callout type="info">
Additional characters require additional Creation Tokens. Your remaining Account Wallet balance stays available for future characters.
</Callout>
