---
title: Battle Pass
description: Seasonal Battle Pass with free and premium reward tracks.
---

The Battle Pass runs on **seasonal cycles** with two reward tracks. Available in **Nexus**.

Each season lasts **90 days** with **80 tiers** of rewards. Progress by earning XP from any activity.

<Callout type="info">
**Bonus:** Purchasing Battle Pass includes **1 week of Premium Status**.
</Callout>

---

## Reward Tracks

| Track | Cost | Rewards |
|-------|------|---------|
| **Free Track** | Free | Gold, consumables, basic cosmetics |
| **Premium Track** | $2.99 | Exclusive cosmetics, Arcanite, boosters |
