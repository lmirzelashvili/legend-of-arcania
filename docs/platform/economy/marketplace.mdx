The **Marketplace** is Arcania's trading hub, accessible from the Nexus. Buy and sell items for **Gold** or **Arcanite**.

---

## Trading Methods

Arcania supports two trading methods:

| Method | Location | Fees | Best For |
|--------|----------|------|----------|
| **Auction House** | Nexus | 5% (0% Premium) | Selling to the market |
| **Direct Trade** | Nexus + Safe Zones | None | Trading with specific players |

### Listing an Item

1. Open **Marketplace** from Nexus
2. Select item from your **Bag** or **Vault**
3. Choose **currency** (Gold or Arcanite)
4. Set your **price**
5. Confirm listing

### Listing Fees

| Player Type | Sale Fee | Listing Fee |
|-------------|----------|-------------|
| Free Player | 5% of sale price | None |
| Premium | 0% | None |

<Callout type="tip">
**Premium Benefit:** Premium subscribers pay no marketplace fees, keeping 100% of their sales. This is one of the best value features of Premium.
</Callout>

### Canceling Listings

You can cancel a listing at any time. The item returns to your Bag/Vault with no fee.

## Searching & Buying

### Search Filters

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| **Category** | Weapons, Armor, Accessories, Materials, etc. |
| **Class** | Paladin, Warrior, Mage, Cleric, Ranger |
| **Tier** | T1, T2, T3, T4, T5 |
| **Quality** | Standard, Prestige |
| **Price Range** | Min/Max Gold |
| **Stats** | Filter by specific stat bonuses |
| **Enhancement** | +0 through +15 |

### Sort Options

| Sort | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Price (LowвҶ’High) | Cheapest first |
| Price (HighвҶ’Low) | Most expensive first |
| Recently Listed | Newest listings |
| Most Relevant | Best match for search |

### Buying Process

1. Find item using Search/Browse
2. Review item details and stats
3. Click **Buy Now**
4. Confirm purchase (Gold deducted)
5. Item delivered to your Bag

<Callout type="info">
**Instant Delivery:** Purchased items are delivered immediately to your Bag. Make sure you have space!
</Callout>

### Trade Window Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Item slots** | Add up to 12 items |
| **Gold field** | Add gold to offer |
| **Lock button** | Lock your offer for review |
| **Confirm button** | Final confirmation (both must click) |
| **Cancel button** | Abort trade at any time |

### Trade Safety

- Both parties must **Lock** their offers
- Both parties must **Confirm** the final trade
- Trade only completes when both confirm
- Items are exchanged atomically (all or nothing)

<Callout type="warning">
**Scam Prevention:** Always verify what you're receiving before clicking Confirm. The lock phase lets you review the other player's offer.
</Callout>

## Marketplace Etiquette

### Pricing Your Items

| Item Type | Pricing Strategy |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Common items** | Check lowest current price, match or undercut |
| **Rare items** | Check recent sales history, price competitively |
| **Unique items** | Premium pricing acceptable |
| **Materials** | Price per unit, check market rates |

### Price Checking

Before listing:
1. Search for identical/similar items
2. Note current prices
3. Check "Sold" history (if available)
4. Price competitively
