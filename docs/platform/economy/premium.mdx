## Benefits

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **+20% XP gain** | Faster leveling |
| **+10% Gold drops** | More gold from monsters |
| **+2% Drop chance** | Better loot from monsters and events |
| **+5% Enhancement success** | Better upgrade chances |
| **+5% Forging success** | Better crafting chances |
| **0% Marketplace fees** | Keep 100% of sale price |
| **50 listing slots** | vs 20 for free players |

<Callout type="tip">
The crafting bonuses stack additively with crystal bonuses. A +5% forging boost on a 40% base rate becomes 45%.
</Callout>

## How to Purchase

1. Open **Nexus**
2. Go to **Premium** tab
3. Select duration (1 Day, 1 Week, or 1 Month)
4. Complete payment
5. Benefits activate immediately

<Callout type="info">
Premium time stacks. Purchasing while active adds to your remaining time.
</Callout>
