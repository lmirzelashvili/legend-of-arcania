---
title: Vendors
description: NPC shops for equipment, consumables, and premium items.
---

NPC vendors sell items for **Gold** or **Arcanite**. Available in **Nexus** and **in-game Safe Zones**.

---

## Vendor Types

| Vendor | Items | Currency | Location |
|--------|-------|----------|----------|
| **Blacksmith** | Weapons, armor, shields | Gold | Nexus + Safe Zones |
| **Alchemist** | Potions, elixirs, scrolls | Gold | Nexus + Safe Zones |
| **Arcanist** | Cosmetics, boosters, storage | Arcanite | Nexus only |

---

## Blacksmith

Sells low-grade equipment for all classes:

| Category | Items |
|----------|-------|
| **Weapons** | Basic swords, staves, bows |
| **Armor** | Basic chest, helm, gloves, boots |
| **Shields** | Basic shields |

---

## Alchemist

Sells consumables and magic supplies:

| Category | Items |
|----------|-------|
| **Potions** | HP, Mana, buff potions |
| **Elixirs** | Stat boosts, resistances |
| **Scrolls** | Teleport, identify, utility |

---

## Arcanist <img src="/game-icons/arcanite.png" alt="Arcanite" className="inline w-5 h-5 align-middle" />

Premium vendor available only in Nexus. Accepts Arcanite only.

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Aura Effects | 200-500 |
| Title Colors | 50-150 |
| Chat Emotes | 25-100 |

| Item | Cost | Duration | Effect |
