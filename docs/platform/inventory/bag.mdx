### Dual Access

The Bag can be accessed from both the Nexus and In-Game:

| Access Point | Features |
|--------------|----------|
| **Nexus** | Full management, sorting, filtering, Vault transfers |
| **In-Game** | Quick access, drag-to-equip, mid-combat swaps |

<Callout type="info">
Opening your Bag in-game does **not** pause the game. Be careful opening inventory in combat - you're still vulnerable!
</Callout>

## Risk Mechanics

### Items at Risk

Items in your Bag are **at risk** when you die in **PvP zones**:

| Zone Type | Death Penalty |
|-----------|---------------|
| рҹҹў Safe Zone | No death possible |
| рҹҹЎ PvE Zone | 10% durability loss (no item drops) |
| рҹҹ  Hard PvE Zone | 10% durability loss (no item drops) |
| рҹ”ҙ PvP Zone | **Random items drop from Bag** |

### How Item Drops Work

When you die in a PvP zone:

1. **Equipped items are SAFE** - they stay with you
2. **Random Bag items drop** - selection is random
3. **Drop quantity** - typically 3-5 items depending on Bag contents
4. **Dropped items are lootable** - the player who killed you (or anyone nearby) can pick them up

## Item Safety Rules

### Always Safe (Never Drops)

| Item Type | Why Safe |
|-----------|----------|
| **Equipped gear** | Worn items protected |
| **Quest items** | Cannot be traded or dropped |
| **Bound items** | Soul-bound items protected |

### At Risk in PvP Zones

| Item Type | Risk Level |
|-----------|------------|
| **Unequipped gear** | Can drop |
| **Materials** | Can drop |
| **Gold** | Portion can drop |
| **Consumables** | Can drop |
| **Trade goods** | Can drop |

<Callout type="tip">
**Risk Management:** Before entering PvP zones, equip your best gear (safe), store valuables in Vault, and only carry consumables you're willing to lose.
</Callout>

## Transfer to/from Vault

### Depositing to Vault (Bag вҶ’ Vault)

1. Return to a **Safe Zone**
2. Exit to **Nexus**
3. Open **Inventory Management**
4. Select items from **Bag** tab
5. Click **Transfer to Vault**
6. Items are now safe

### Withdrawing from Vault (Vault вҶ’ Bag)

1. Be in the **Nexus** (not in a zone)
2. Open **Inventory Management**
3. Select items from **Vault** tab
4. Choose character to receive
5. Click **Transfer to Bag**
6. Items are now at risk when entering zones

<Callout type="warning">
**Cannot transfer in Danger Zones!** If you're deep in a Dominion territory with valuable loot, you must survive the journey back to a Safe Zone before you can deposit to Vault.
</Callout>

## Strategic Considerations

### Before Entering PvP Zones

**Recommended Bag Contents:**
- Equipped best gear (safe)
- Health potions (replaceable)
- Mana potions (replaceable)
- Buff scrolls (worth the risk)
- Minimal gold (can drop)

**Leave in Vault:**
- Backup equipment
- Rare materials
- Large gold reserves
- Items for other characters

### The Loot Decision

When you find valuable loot in a dangerous zone, you face a choice:

```
Found rare drop! Options:
в”Ӯ
в”ңв”Җв”Җ Continue deeper (more rewards, more risk)

в”ңв”Җв”Җ Return to Safe Zone now (secure the loot)
```

<Callout type="tip">
**Pro Strategy:** When you find something valuable, consider making a "loot run" back to safety. The extra trip is worth securing rare items.
</Callout>

## Death Recovery

### PvE Death (Durability Loss)

In PvE zones, death only costs durability:
- 10% durability loss on all equipped items
- No items drop from Bag
- Respawn at nearest Safe Zone

### PvP Death (Item Drop)

In PvP zones, death is more punishing:
- Random items drop from Bag (3-5 items typically)
- Dropped items appear on ground
- **Killer can loot your drops** (and anyone else nearby)
- Respawn at nearest Safe Zone
- Equipped items are safe

<Callout type="info">
**Corpse Run:** You can attempt to return to where you died to recover dropped items - but they may already be taken by your killer or other players.
</Callout>
