Arcania uses a **dual inventory system**: a safe account-wide **Vault** and a character **Bag** that can drop items on death.

---

## Item Safety Hierarchy

Items have different levels of protection depending on where they're stored:

| Location | Safety | Description |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| **Vault** | 100% Safe | Never lost under any circumstances |
| **Equipped** | Safe on Death | Worn items stay with you even when killed |
| **Bag** | At Risk | Can be lost when dying in PvP zones |
| **Ground** | Lootable | Dropped items can be picked up by anyone |

<Callout type="info">
**Key Insight:** Store valuable items you're not using in the Vault. Only carry what you're willing to risk in your Bag when venturing into dangerous zones.
</Callout>

---

## Transferring Items

Items can be moved between Vault and Bag from the Nexus:

### Vault → Bag (Before adventuring)
1. Open Inventory Management in Nexus
2. Select items from Vault
3. Choose destination character
4. Confirm transfer
5. Items appear in character's Bag

### Bag → Vault (After adventuring)
1. Return to a Safe Zone
2. Exit to Nexus
3. Open Inventory Management
4. Select items from Bag
5. Transfer to Vault for safekeeping

<Callout type="warning">
**Cannot transfer while in Danger Zones!** You must reach a Safe Zone before accessing the Vault or transferring items.
</Callout>
