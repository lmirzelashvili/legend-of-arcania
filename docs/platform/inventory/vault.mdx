## Access

The Vault is only accessible from the **Nexus**. You cannot access it while in-game.

---

## Capacity

| Tier | Slots | Unlock |
|------|-------|--------|
| **Base** | 100 | Default |
| **Expanded** | 150 | <img src="/game-icons/gold.png" alt="Gold" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 50,000 Gold |
| **Premium (Battle Pass)** | 200 | Premium Subscription |

Upgrades stack: Base (100) + Gold (+50) + Premium (+50) = 200 slots.

---

## Organization

**Sort by:** Type, Tier, Class, Recent, Name

**Filter by:** Search, Type, Class, Tier

---

## Transfers

To transfer items from Vault to a character's Bag:
1. Select items in Vault
2. Choose destination character
3. Confirm transfer

**Requirements:**
- Character must have Bag space
- Character cannot be in a Danger Zone

---

## Best Practices

**Store in Vault:** High-tier gear, enhancement materials, rare crafting materials, alt gear, large gold amounts

**Keep in Bag:** Potions, common materials, items to sell
