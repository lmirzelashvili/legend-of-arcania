## Referral Quests

Invite friends and earn rewards when they progress.

| Quest | Requirement | Reward |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| First Referral | 1 referred player reaches level 10 | <img src="/game-icons/arcanite.png" alt="Arcanite" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 10 Arcanite |
| Recruiter | 5 referred players reach level 10 | <img src="/game-icons/arcanite.png" alt="Arcanite" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 50 Arcanite |
| Ambassador | 10 referred players reach level 10 | <img src="/game-icons/arcanite.png" alt="Arcanite" className="inline w-4 h-4 align-middle" /> 100 Arcanite + Box of Inferno |

### Referral Terms

- Referred player must reach **level 10** for reward to unlock
- Referred player must have **5+ hours playtime**
- One reward per unique referred account
- Self-referrals and duplicate accounts do not qualify
- Rewards granted after 7-day verification period
