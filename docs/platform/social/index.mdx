## Quick Overview

### Friends

Add players to your friends list for:
- See online status
- Quick party invites
- Direct messaging

### Party System

Form groups of 4-5 players:
- Shared XP (within level range)
- Coordinated combat
- Party chat channel
- Loot distribution options

See [Party System](/docs/gameplay/party-system) for full details.
