Participate in chat-based events for Gold, items, and Arcanite rewards.

---

## Events

| Event | How It Works | Rewards |
|-------|--------------|---------|
| **Chat Drops** | Random drops for active chatters | Gold, items, Arcanite |
| **Trivia** | Answer questions first | Gold, Arcanite |
| **Word Scramble** | Unscramble names first | Gold, Arcanite |

---

## Chat Drops

Random rewards drop in chat for active participants.

- Stay active in chat to be eligible
- Drops announced in All chat
- Click to claim before others

---

## Trivia

Scheduled trivia questions posted in chat.

- Lore, game knowledge, and general questions
- First correct answer wins
- Multiple rounds per session

---

## Word Scramble

Scrambled item, monster, or zone names posted in chat.

- First to type the correct answer wins
- Difficulty varies (common to rare names)
- Quick thinking rewarded
