Arcania's world is divided into different zone types, each with distinct rules for safety, combat, and death penalties. Understanding these zones is crucial for survival.

## 🟢 Safe Zones

**No danger. Full services. Nexus accessible.**

Safe Zones are protected areas where combat is disabled and you can safely manage your character.

### Characteristics

| Feature | Status |
|---------|--------|
| Monster spawns | None |
| PvP | Disabled |
| Death penalty | N/A (cannot die) |
| Nexus access | Yes - can exit to Nexus |
| Services | All NPCs, vendors, facilities |

### Examples

| Zone | Location |
|------|----------|
| <TerritoryMention territory="valoryn">Valoryn</TerritoryMention> Capital | Heart of the kingdom |
| <TerritoryMention territory="meridia">Meridia</TerritoryMention> Capital | Coastal trading hub |
| <TerritoryMention territory="lythora">Lythora</TerritoryMention> Capital | Forest sanctuary |
| <TerritoryMention territory="redmire">Redmire</TerritoryMention> Capital | Mountain stronghold |
| <TerritoryMention territory="lumeria">Lumeria</TerritoryMention> Capital | Arcane city |
| Outposts | Various safe points in wilderness |

### Available in Safe Zones

- **Vault Access** - Transfer items safely
- **NPC Vendors** - Buy/sell items
- **Forgemaster** - Crafting station
- **Quest NPCs** - Accept and turn in quests
- **Exit to Nexus** - Safe logout

<Callout type="info">
You can only exit to the Nexus from Safe Zones. Attempting to logout elsewhere will result in your character remaining in the world (and potentially dying).
</Callout>

## 🟡 PvE Danger Zones

**Monsters present. Durability loss on death. No PvP.**

Most of the world falls into this category - wilderness areas where monsters roam but players cannot attack each other.

### Characteristics

| Feature | Status |
|---------|--------|
| Monster spawns | Yes |
| PvP | Disabled |
| Death penalty | **40% durability loss** |
| Nexus access | No - must return to Safe Zone |
| Services | Limited (some outposts have vendors) |

### Examples

| Zone | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Free Realm Wilderness | Forests, plains, caves outside cities |
| Low-level Dungeons | Instanced or open-world monster areas |
| Resource gathering areas | Mining, herbalism, hunting grounds |

### Death in PvE Zones

When you die to monsters in a PvE zone:

1. **40% durability loss** on all equipped items
2. **Respawn** at nearest Safe Zone or outpost
3. **10-second debuff** - Resurrection Sickness (reduced stats)
4. **No item drops** - Bag contents are safe

<Callout type="warning">
**Item Destruction:** If durability reaches 0%, the item is **permanently destroyed**. Two deaths without repairing (40% + 40% = 80%) leaves you dangerously close to losing your gear!
</Callout>

<Callout type="tip">
Always repair your equipment at a Blacksmith NPC in Safe Zones. The gold cost is much cheaper than losing your items forever.
</Callout>

## 🟠 Hard PvE Zones

**Extremely dangerous monsters. SEVERE durability loss on death. No PvP.**

The deep Dominion territories and challenging dungeons fall into this category - deadly PvE content without the added threat of other players. Death here is especially punishing.

### Characteristics

| Feature | Status |
|---------|--------|
| Monster spawns | Yes - elite and boss monsters |
| PvP | Disabled |
| Death penalty | **80% durability loss** |
| Nexus access | No - must return to Safe Zone |
| Services | None or very limited |

### Examples

| Zone | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Deep <TerritoryMention territory="dreadmar">Dreadmar</TerritoryMention> | Inner corrupted territories |
| <TerritoryMention territory="stonegrave">Stonegrave</TerritoryMention> Depths | Underground dominion areas |
| Boss Chambers | Dominion General lairs |
| High-tier Dungeons | Endgame instanced content |

### Why Hard PvE?

Some Dominion territories are designated Hard PvE rather than PvP to:
- Allow challenging content without grief potential
- Enable group PvE progression
- Provide "hardcore but fair" experiences

<Callout type="warning">
**80% Durability Loss:** One death to a boss brings your gear to 20% durability. A second death would **destroy all your equipment permanently**. Come prepared with full repairs and don't underestimate these zones!
</Callout>

## 🔴 PvP Zones

**Players can attack you. Items drop on death. Maximum risk, maximum reward.**

Contested territories where players can freely attack each other. Death here means potentially losing items from your Bag to your killer.

### Characteristics

| Feature | Status |
|---------|--------|
| Monster spawns | Yes |
| PvP | Enabled - players can attack |
| Death penalty | Random item drops from Bag |
| Nexus access | No - must return to Safe Zone |
| Services | None (hostile territory) |

### Examples

| Zone | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Contested Dominion borders | Frontier areas between realms |
| <TerritoryMention territory="eclipse">Eclipse Barrens</TerritoryMention> | Lawless wasteland |
| <TerritoryMention territory="mire">Mire of Whispers</TerritoryMention> edges | Corrupted borderlands |
| Resource hotspots | Rare material spawns attract conflict |

### Death in PvP Zones

When you die in a PvP zone:

1. **Random items drop** from your Bag (3-5 items typically)
2. **Dropped items are lootable** by anyone
3. **Equipped items are SAFE** - they stay with you
4. **Respawn** at nearest Safe Zone
5. **Resurrection Sickness** - 10-second reduced stats

### The PvP Risk/Reward

| Risk | Reward |
|------|--------|
| Can lose Bag items | Rare resource spawns |
| Other players hunting | Exclusive material drops |
| Long distance from safety | PvP ranking points |
| No services nearby | Excitement and adrenaline |

<Callout type="warning">
**Exit Warning:** If you try to exit to Nexus while in a PvP zone, your character will be **killed** instantly. Always return to a Safe Zone before logging out!
</Callout>

## Zone Transitions

### Safe → Danger Zone

When leaving a Safe Zone:

```
┌──────────────┐     ┌───────────────────────────────────┐
│  SAFE ZONE   │────►│  ⚠️ WARNING: Entering Danger Zone  │
│  (Valoryn)   │     │  PvE Zone: Monsters present       │
└──────────────┘     │  Death penalty: Durability loss   │
                     │  [Enter] [Cancel]                 │
                     └───────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Danger → PvP Zone

When entering a PvP zone:

```
┌──────────────┐     ┌───────────────────────────────────┐
│  PVE ZONE    │────►│  ⚠️ WARNING: Entering PvP Zone     │
│  (Wilderness)│     │  Players CAN attack you           │
└──────────────┘     │  Death penalty: ITEM DROPS        │
                     │  [Enter at your own risk] [Back]  │
                     └───────────────────────────────────┘
```

<Callout type="info">
Zone boundaries are clearly marked on the map with colored borders. Green = Safe, Yellow = PvE, Orange = Hard PvE, Red = PvP.
</Callout>

## Zone Map Integration

The world map shows zone classifications:

| Map Color | Zone Type | Risk Level |
|-----------|-----------|------------|
| 🟢 Green | Safe Zone | None |
| 🟡 Yellow | PvE Zone | Low |
| 🟠 Orange | Hard PvE Zone | Medium |
| 🔴 Red | PvP Zone | High |

### Reading the Map

- **Solid colors** indicate zone type
- **Hatched areas** indicate contested/changing zones
- **Icons** show available services (vendors, forgemasters)
- **Skull icons** indicate boss locations

## Strategic Considerations

### Choosing Your Path

| Goal | Recommended Zones |
|------|-------------------|
| Leveling safely | 🟡 PvE zones matching your level |
| Challenging content | 🟠 Hard PvE zones |
| Best materials | 🔴 PvP zones (if you can handle the risk) |
| Trading/Crafting | 🟢 Safe zones |

### PvP Zone Survival Tips

1. **Travel light** - Only carry what you're willing to lose
2. **Stay alert** - Watch for other players
3. **Know your exits** - Map escape routes to Safe Zones
4. **Group up** - Safety in numbers
5. **Deposit often** - Make frequent loot runs back to safety
