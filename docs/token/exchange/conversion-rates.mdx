Your Arcanite to ARC conversion rate is determined by two factors: your **Account Rate** (based on progression) and your **Time-Lock Rate** (based on withdrawal speed).

---

## Base Conversion

**100 Arcanite = 1 ARC Token** at 100% combined rate.

Your actual rate is calculated as:

```
Final Rate = Account Rate × Time-Lock Rate
ARC Received = (Arcanite ÷ 100) × Final Rate
```

---

## Account Rate

Your Account Rate scales from **40% to 100%** based on three weighted factors:

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **Level** | 30% | Progress toward max level (85) |
| **Playtime** | 20% | Total hours played (over 30+ days) |
| **Achievements** | 50% | Quests, milestones, and progression |

### Level Contribution (30% weight)

| Level Range | Contribution |
|-------------|--------------|
| 35-45 | 0-8% |
| 46-55 | 8-15% |
| 56-70 | 15-22% |
| 71-84 | 22-28% |
| 85 (Max) | 30% |

### Playtime Contribution (20% weight)

| Hours Played | Contribution |
|--------------|--------------|
| 100-200 | 0-5% |
| 201-400 | 5-10% |
| 401-700 | 10-15% |
| 701-1000 | 15-18% |
| 1000+ | 20% |

<Callout type="info">
Playtime must be accumulated over a minimum of **30 calendar days**. This prevents rapid farming and ensures genuine engagement.
</Callout>

### Achievement Contribution (50% weight)

| Achievement Progress | Contribution |
|---------------------|--------------|
| Basic homeland quests | 0-10% |
| All main story quests | 10-20% |
| Nexus Quests completed | 20-30% |
| All quests + Social Quests | 30-40% |
| All achievements + Rare milestones | 50% |

Achievements carry the highest weight because they require genuine gameplay skill and engagement that bots cannot easily replicate.

- Level 85 (max level)
- All main story quests completed
- All Nexus Quests completed
- All Social Quests completed

## Time-Lock Rates

Choose how quickly you receive your ARC tokens:

| Withdrawal Type | Rate | Delivery Time | Dynamic? |
|-----------------|------|---------------|----------|
| **Instant** | 30-60% | Immediate | Yes — Oracle adjusted |
| **1 Week Lock** | 60-80% | 7 days | Yes — Oracle adjusted |
| **2 Week Lock** | 100% | 14 days | **No — Always 100%** |

**Normal rates:** Instant 50% / 1-Week 75% / 2-Week 100%

### Why Time-Locks?

Time-locks serve multiple purposes:

- **Reduces selling pressure** — Spreads withdrawals over time
- **Rewards patience** — Dedicated players always get the best rate
- **Discourages bots** — Extraction efficiency reduced
- **Price stability** — Prevents sudden supply dumps

<Callout type="tip">
The **2-week lock rate is always 100%** regardless of Oracle state. If you're patient, you always get the best deal.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Cancellation:** Pending withdrawals can be cancelled anytime before completion for a full Arcanite refund.
</Callout>

## Dynamic Rate Oracle

The Oracle adjusts **only instant and 1-week rates** plus **weekly caps** based on economic health. Fixed parameters (base rate, account formula, 2-week rate, fees) never change.

### What the Oracle Controls

| Component | Normal | Stressed | Growing | Hard Range |
|-----------|--------|----------|---------|------------|
| **Instant Rate** | 50% | 30% | 60% | 30-60% |
| **1-Week Rate** | 75% | 60% | 80% | 60-80% |
| **Weekly Cap (Basic)** | 10,000 | 5,000 | 15,000 | 5k-15k |
| **Weekly Cap (KYC)** | 20,000 | 10,000 | 25,000 | 10k-25k |

### What Triggers Adjustments

| Indicator | Oracle Response |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Extraction rate over 50% of earned | Decrease instant/1-week rates, tighten caps |
| Player base declining 3+ months | Decrease rates |
| Healthy growth + low extraction | Maintain or increase |
| Token price stable/growing | Maintain |

### Oracle Rules

- Reviewed every **Monday at 00:00 UTC**
- Changes announced **7 days in advance**
- Published ranges are **hard limits** — Oracle cannot exceed them
- Current values always visible in Exchange interface

<Callout type="warning">
During economic stress, instant withdrawals may yield as low as 30%. The 2-week lock is always 100% — patience is always rewarded.
</Callout>

## Rate Optimization Tips

1. **Focus on achievements** - Largest contribution (50%), requires genuine gameplay
2. **Complete all quest lines** - Required for maximum rate
3. **Always use 2-week lock** - Double the rate of instant withdrawals
4. **Be patient** - Don't rush conversions at low rates
5. **Spread playtime naturally** - 30-day minimum prevents rushed farming
