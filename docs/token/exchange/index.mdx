The Arcanite Exchange is the bridge between in-game earnings and blockchain value. Convert your hard-earned Arcanite into ARC Token, Arcania's cryptocurrency with real-world value.

---

## How It Works

1. **Earn Arcanite** through gameplay, achievements, and events
2. **Meet Requirements** - Level 35+, 100 hours over 30+ days, verification
3. **Choose Conversion** - Select amount and time-lock option (weekly caps apply)
4. **Receive ARC** - Tokens delivered to your connected wallet

---

## Base Conversion Rate

<div className="text-center py-6 bg-muted rounded-lg my-4">
  <p className="text-3xl font-bold">100 Arcanite = 1 ARC Token</p>
  <p className="text-sm text-muted-foreground mt-2">At 100% account rate</p>
</div>

Your actual conversion rate depends on:
- **Account Rate** (40-100%) - Based on progression
- **Time-Lock Rate** (30-100%) - Based on withdrawal speed (instant and 1-week rates are Oracle-adjusted)

**Final Rate = Account Rate × Time-Lock Rate**

---

## Quick Example

| Player Type | Account Rate | Time-Lock | Final Rate | 1000 Arcanite = |
|-------------|--------------|-----------|------------|-----------------|
| New (Lv40) | 40% | Instant (50%*) | 20% | 2 ARC |
| Mid (Lv60) | 55% | 1 Week (75%*) | 41% | 4.1 ARC |
| Veteran (Lv85) | 100% | 2 Weeks (100%) | 100% | 10 ARC |

*\*Instant and 1-week rates shown at normal values. These are Oracle-adjusted within published ranges (instant 30-60%, 1-week 60-80%). The 2-week lock rate is **always 100%** regardless of Oracle state.*

<Callout type="tip">
Patient veteran players receive **5x more ARC** than rushing new players for the same Arcanite amount. Weekly caps are Oracle-adjusted: 5k-15k Arcanite (basic) / 10k-25k (KYC verified).
</Callout>

## Why This System?

The exchange system is designed to:

| Goal | How Achieved |
|------|--------------|
| **Reward Dedication** | Higher rates for veteran players |
| **Discourage Extraction** | 3% fee for instant, 2% for time-locked |
| **Prevent Bots** | L35 + 100h/30 days + weekly caps |
| **Maintain Value** | All fees burned to reduce supply |
| **Economic Balance** | Dynamic rate adjustments based on health |

<Callout type="info">
Pending withdrawals can be cancelled anytime for a full Arcanite refund. Only completed withdrawals incur fees.
</Callout>
