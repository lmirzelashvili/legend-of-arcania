The Arcanite Exchange has requirements designed to ensure only genuine players can convert to ARC tokens. These gates prevent bot exploitation while remaining achievable for dedicated players.

---

## Basic Requirements

| Requirement | Minimum Value |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Character Level** | 35 |
| **Total Playtime** | 100 hours over 30+ calendar days |
| **Minimum Conversion** | 1,000 Arcanite |
| **Wallet Connection** | Required |
| **Account Verification** | Email + Captcha |
| **Account Creation** | $0.99 one-time fee |

<Callout type="info">
All requirements must be met simultaneously. You cannot convert if any single requirement is unmet.
</Callout>

---

## Level Requirement

**Minimum: Level 35**

Reaching Level 35 typically requires:
- Completing homeland quests
- Progressing into Valoryn content
- Significant combat experience
- Solid understanding of game mechanics

This ensures converters have genuinely invested time in the game rather than creating accounts purely for extraction.

---

## Playtime Requirement

**Minimum: 100 hours over 30+ calendar days**

The playtime requirement has two components:

| Component | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Total Hours** | 100 hours minimum |
| **Calendar Days** | Spread over 30+ different days |

This dual requirement:
- Tracks total /played time across all sessions
- Does not count AFK time (logged out after 15 min idle)
- Accumulates across all characters on account
- **Prevents rapid farming** by requiring time spread over a month

<Callout type="warning">
Playtime manipulation (staying logged in while not playing) is detected and may result in account restrictions.
</Callout>

## Verification Tiers

### Tier 1: Basic Verification

**Requirements:**
- Email verification
- Captcha completion
- Wallet connection
- $0.99 account creation fee paid

**Limits:**
- Up to **$250** cumulative withdrawals
- Standard conversion rates
- **10,000 Arcanite** weekly cap (Oracle-adjusted: 5k-15k)

### Tier 2: KYC Verification

**Requirements:**
- Government-issued ID
- Selfie verification
- Address confirmation

**Limits:**
- Unlimited withdrawal value
- Priority processing
- **20,000 Arcanite** weekly cap (Oracle-adjusted: 10k-25k)
- Access to enhanced features

<Callout type="info">
**Why KYC at $250?** Regulatory compliance requires identity verification for financial transactions. The $250 threshold demonstrates proactive compliance while allowing casual players to convert small amounts without friction.
</Callout>

## Verification Process

### Basic Verification Steps

1. **Account Creation ($0.99)**
   - One-time fee during account creation
   - Provides initial bot deterrent
   - Required for all accounts

2. **Email Verification**
   - Confirm email address
   - Check spam folder if no email received
   - One-time verification per account

3. **Captcha Completion**
   - Solve verification challenge
   - Required once per conversion session
   - Prevents automated submissions

4. **Wallet Connection**
   - Connect supported Web3 wallet
   - Sign verification message (gas-free)
   - Wallet becomes permanent receiving address

### KYC Verification Steps

**Required once cumulative conversions reach $250**

1. **Submit Documents**
   - Government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's license, national ID)
   - Clear, unobstructed photos of document

2. **Selfie Verification**
   - Live selfie matching ID photo
   - Liveness detection (blink, turn head)
   - Prevents photo spoofing

3. **Address Verification**
   - Utility bill or bank statement
   - Must match ID address
   - Document within 3 months

**Processing Time:** 1-3 business days

## Troubleshooting

### "Requirements Not Met"

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Level too low | Continue playing to reach Level 35 |
| Insufficient playtime | Accumulate 100+ hours over 30+ days |
| Not enough calendar days | Keep playing daily to reach 30 days |
| Email not verified | Check email and verify |
| Wallet not connected | Connect Web3 wallet |

### "Conversion Failed"

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Insufficient Arcanite | Need minimum 1,000 Arcanite |
| Weekly cap reached | Wait for cap reset (Monday 00:00 UTC) |
| KYC required | Complete identity verification (over $250) |
| Wallet error | Disconnect and reconnect wallet |
