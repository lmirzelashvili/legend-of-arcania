## The Tragic Heart

At the center of Arcania's war is a family torn apart by love, loss, and betrayal:

- **Anias** died because a mortal feared losing love
- **Alassa** fell because a mortal betrayed her trust
- **Erion** fights because he promised his dying mother
- **Marael** raged because he lost everything he built

The war is cosmic in scale, but personal in origin. Understanding the characters means understanding why everything else happened.
