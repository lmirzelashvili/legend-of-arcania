## The Land

### Before the Fall
The Eclipse Barrens were once called the **Sunward Plains**-fertile grasslands that received more sunlight than anywhere else in Arcania. Farmers, ranchers, and travelers crisscrossed these lands freely.

### After Corruption
The Anguish Stone's power reached here and **wounded the sky itself**. A permanent eclipse settled over the region-not true darkness, but something worse: eternal half-light that never brightens, never fully darkens. The land withered. The people fled or died. What remains is emptiness given geography.

<Callout type="warning" title="Environmental Hazards">
- **The Half-Light** - Colors fade, perception dims, morale drains
- **Void-Wind** - Strips away magical protections and buffs
- **Despair Zones** - Areas where hope actively attacks the mind
- **The Trembling** - Earth shakes with titan-scarred breath; causes disorientation
</Callout>

## Morgathis, Hand of the Titan

### Origin
Morgathis was not born, transformed, or corrupted-it was **constructed**. When the Eterna Citadel fell, a colossal hand emerged from the dimensional rift and crushed it. That hand did not vanish. Instead, it detached from whatever larger form it belonged to and became Morgathis-an avatar of the Dark Titan's lingering will.

### Nature
Morgathis is not truly alive. It is an **echo of cosmic ambition** given form-the Dark Titan's drive to consume and grow, concentrated into a being of incredible power. It serves Alassa because the Anguish Stone commands it, but some believe it has its own agenda: to serve as a seed from which the Dark Titan might one day return.

## Strategy

### Recommended Party
- **Tank:** Faith-based mitigation essential; physical armor less important
- **Healer:** Clerics excel here; the Eternal Light counters despair
- **DPS:** Holy damage dealers have significant advantage
- **Support:** Buff preservation critical; void-wind strips protections

### Key Tactics
1. **Maintain Faith** - Literally; faith-based abilities counter despair
2. **Interrupt the Hand** - Phase 3's ultimate is unsurvivable if completed
3. **Resist the Whisper** - Party members who fall must be crowd-controlled or killed
4. **Stay Mobile** - Reality Warp punishes static positioning

### Common Mistakes
- Relying on non-faith buffs (void-wind strips them)
- Ignoring "Titan's Whisper" resist checks (turned allies are dangerous)
- Missing the interrupt on "Hand of the Titan" (guaranteed wipe)
- Losing hope (the land feeds on despair; maintain morale)

## Lore Significance

### The Titan's Return?
Morgathis's existence raises terrifying questions. If a hand can separate and become autonomous, what else might emerge? Is the Dark Titan truly dead, or merely scattered? Could it reassemble?

### The Test of Faith
The Eclipse Barrens are where faith is tested most severely. Not combat skill, not tactical acumen-faith. Clerics and Paladins who enter doubt their purpose. Many leave changed. Some don't leave at all.

<Callout type="info">
The Barrens have the highest rate of psychological casualties among the dominions. Warriors who've never shown fear break here. Mages who've mastered the Anguish lose themselves. Only those with something to believe in survive intact.
</Callout>

### The Whisper's Temptation
Morgathis doesn't just attack-it offers. Power. Purpose. An end to struggle. Some who hear the Titan's Whisper accept. They don't die; they simply stop fighting for the resistance. This loss of will is more dangerous than any physical defeat.
