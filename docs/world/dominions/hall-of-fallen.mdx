## The Land

### Before the Fall
The Eterna Citadel was the home of the gods-a divine monument created by Marael and Anias above the mortal realms. When the dimensional rift tore open the sky, a colossal hand crushed it. The broken citadel fell like a dead star.

### Current State
The Hall of Fallen is what remains: fragments of divine architecture floating in unstable space. Rooms that once held gods now drift through void. Corridors connect to impossible places. The rules of reality are suggestions here.

<Callout type="warning" title="Environmental Hazards">
- **Shifting Architecture** - Platforms move, rotate, and vanish
- **Divine Echoes** - Remnants of godly power discharge unpredictably
- **Dimensional Tears** - Portals to elsewhere; some helpful, most deadly
- **Gravity Flux** - Direction of "down" changes without warning
</Callout>

## Cassian the Hollow

### Origin
Cassian was a mortal man-a charismatic army general who captured **Alassa's** heart. Their love was genuine, passionate, and doomed. When Alassa discovered his betrayal with a servant, her heartbreak became rage. She killed the servant and her family, then tore Cassian's heart from his chest-not killing him, but removing his capacity to feel anything.

### Transformation
Cassian wandered the world as a hollow shell-alive but empty, unable to feel love, hate, fear, or hope. When the Anguish Stone's corruption spread, it found his emptiness and filled it with power. He became Alassa's strongest general-bound to the final gate before her throne by whatever trace of their old bond still lingers.

<Callout type="tip">
Cassian's "Emotional Resonance" weakness is unique. Speaking Alassa's name or referencing their history creates damage windows-but also triggers his most dangerous attacks. Use sparingly and strategically.
</Callout>

## Strategy

### Recommended Party
- **Tank:** Must handle Perfect Strike without block; evasion or immunity
- **Healer:** Plan around "Heart of Nothing" phases; burst healing required
- **DPS:** Consistent damage; few burst windows until emotional triggers
- **Support:** Anti-counter mechanics extremely valuable

### Key Tactics
1. **Navigate the Environment** - The floating citadel is as dangerous as Cassian
2. **Trigger Sparingly** - Emotional references create openings but invite devastation
3. **Heal in Bursts** - "Heart of Nothing" means sustained healing doesn't work
4. **Respect His Skill** - Cassian was a legendary general before corruption

### Common Mistakes
- Standing still (platform may vanish)
- Over-using emotional triggers (Final Devotion is devastating)
- Melee attacking during Hollow Guard (counterattack kills)
- Trying to heal through Heart of Nothing (waste of resources)

## Lore Significance

### The Tragedy of Love
Cassian and Alassa's story is central to Arcania's history. Their love, betrayal, and mutual destruction set events in motion that led to the current war. Fighting Cassian means confronting the consequences of passion turned to poison.

### The Empty Guardian
Cassian doesn't guard the path to Alassa because he chooses to. He guards it because he cannot choose anything. The Anguish Stone filled his emptiness with purpose, and that purpose is absolute defense.

<Callout type="info">
Some believe that if Cassian could be restored-if his heart could be returned-he might be turned against Alassa. This theory assumes he would want to turn, which assumes he can want anything at all.
</Callout>

### The Final Test
Defeating Cassian means the Throne of Ruin is finally within reach. But it also means confronting the truth: Alassa's greatest defender is a monument to her capacity for destruction. What waits beyond is not likely to be less dangerous.
