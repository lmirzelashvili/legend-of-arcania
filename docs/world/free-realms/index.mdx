## The Flow of War

```
Homelands → Valoryn → The Five Dominions → Throne of Ruin
```

Every defender begins in their homeland, training in the traditions of their people. When ready, they journey to <TerritoryMention territory="valoryn">Valoryn</TerritoryMention> to join Erion's forces. From there, the campaign pushes through the five corrupted dominions toward Alassa's palace-the fallen Eterna Citadel, now called the **Throne of Ruin**.

## A Land Scarred by Titans

The geography of Arcania bears the marks of cosmic forces:

- **The Anguish Scars** - Rifts where Titan energy still bleeds through
- **The Corruption Spread** - Lands twisted by the Anguish Stone's influence
- **The First World Remnants** - Ancient forests from Anias's dreaming, before she awoke
- **The Divine Echoes** - Places where gods walked, still resonant with power

<Callout type="info">
The Free Lands represent roughly 30% of Arcania's original territory. The rest has fallen to Alassa's dominion-but the resistance grows stronger with each passing year.
</Callout>
