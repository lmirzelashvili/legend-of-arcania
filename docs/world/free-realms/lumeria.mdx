## Origin of Lumeria

When Anias died, her body dissolved into light-a blinding flash that illuminated the entire realm. Most of this radiance dispersed across the cosmos, but some concentrated in a single region, crystallizing over decades into formations that still glow with divine essence.

The Luminar, who are themselves fragments of Anias's consciousness, felt drawn here irresistibly. They built their civilization around these crystals, believing that proximity to their mother's crystallized light brings them closer to her presence.

<Callout type="info">
Lumeria's crystals are not inert. They pulse with a slow rhythm-what the Luminar call "Mother's Heartbeat." Some believe Anias's consciousness lingers within, dreaming still.
</Callout>

## Geography

### The Crystal Fields
Vast plains where crystalline formations grow like forests. Each crystal glows faintly, creating a landscape of soft, eternal twilight. Walking here is considered a meditation.

### The Radiant Spire
The Luminar capital, built around and within the largest crystal formation in Arcania. The spire rises a thousand feet, visible for hundreds of miles. Within, the Seraph Council governs and the most sacred rituals are performed.

### The Vigil Shrines
Smaller communities scattered across Lumeria, each maintaining a **Vigil Flame**-a sacred fire representing Anias's undying light. These shrines serve as temples, hospitals, and training centers.

### The Cathedral of Light
Built where Anias actually died-not in Lumeria itself, but accessible via sacred pathways. Luminar make pilgrimage here to feel closest to their mother's lingering essence.

### The Seraph Council
Lumeria is ruled by the eldest and wisest Luminar, who make decisions through consensus. **High Seraph Aurelia** currently leads, though she carries burdens she shares with no one.

### The Grief That Never Fades
Luminar are immortal. Those who lived during Anias's time still remember her death vividly. This grief shapes their culture-a deep sadness that never fully heals, but which they transform into purpose.

### Temples of Light
Luminar live in **temples** rather than cities. These sacred spaces serve as homes, governments, and places of worship. Architecture emphasizes openness, light, and crystal.

<Callout type="warning" title="The Dimmed">
Some Luminar have fallen to shadow-transformed by exposure to void essence or succumbing to despair. They are called the **Dimmed**, and they are hunted by their former kin. The orthodox view them as traitors. The compassionate see them as victims. The wise wonder if the line between light and shadow is as clear as they were taught.
</Callout>

Luminar have the highest starting Spirit in the game, making them exceptional for any magic-focused build. Their low Strength is easily compensated by their natural power.
</Callout>

## The Path to Valoryn

From Lumeria, the journey to Valoryn is relatively short, aided by ley-line travel:

1. **The Crystal Roads** - Sacred paths connecting major shrines
2. **The Ley Crossing** - Magical shortcut through crystallized energy
3. **The Western March** - Final stretch to Valoryn's walls
4. **Valoryn's Crystal Gate** - Maintained by Luminar wardkeepers

The journey takes only 2-3 weeks-the shortest of any homeland-thanks to ley-line assistance.

## Notable Locations

### The Meditation Crystals
Perfect crystalline formations where Luminar go to commune with their inner light. Extended meditation here can produce visions of Anias's life.

### The Healing Sanctum
The premier medical facility in Arcania, where the most grievous wounds can be mended. The Eternal Light burns brightest here.

### The Archive of Light
Repository of all knowledge from before the Duskfall. Luminar scribes work tirelessly to preserve every text, every scroll, every fragment of the world that was.

### The Sister's Garden
A sacred grove where Luminar and Lilin meet to celebrate their sisterly bond-the conscious light and unconscious dreams of their shared mother Anias. The garden blends crystal formations with living trees, bridging both worlds.
