## Origin of Redmire

When the Dark Titan was destroyed, its physical form scattered across the cosmos. Some of this matter-dense, powerful, and strangely alive-eventually coalesced into the Darkan. They emerged from deep beneath the surface, in places of immense pressure and heat.

Redmire sits above the largest concentration of **Titan Matter** in Arcania. The Darkan settled here not just for the mineral wealth, but because the land itself resonates with their origin. They feel the mountain's heartbeat, the singing of metals, the memory of being molten rock.

<Callout type="info">
The name "Redmire" comes from the iron-rich soil that colors the mountain slopes. When it rains, the runoff turns streams red-as if the mountains themselves bleed.
</Callout>

Unlike common belief, Darkan don't only live underground. Their surface fortresses guard mountain passes and watch the northern approaches. These structures are architectural marvels-built to withstand siege, avalanche, and even earthquake.

### Stoneheart City
The Darkan capital, carved from the heart of Mount Stonejaw. Every surface is worked stone-pillars depicting ancient heroes, walls inscribed with the Forge Creed, floors polished to mirror brightness. It is said that ten thousand years of labor went into its creation.

### The Great Forges
Massive smithies powered by volcanic vents, where the finest weapons and armor in Arcania are produced. Volcanic steel and Titanbone alloys originate here.

## Culture

### The Forge Creed
The philosophical foundation of Darkan society:

> "From raw stone, I was carved.
> Through fire, I was tested.
> By pressure, I was strengthened.
> My word is mountain.
> My will is iron.
> I am Darkan.
> I am forged."

### Clan Structure
Darkan organize into clans, each led by a **Stone Father** or **Stone Mother**. The High King is elected from among clan leaders and serves until death or failure.

### The Oath Binding
A Darkan's word is considered literally unbreakable. Oaths physically bind them-breaking an oath causes genuine spiritual damage. This trait makes them the most trusted diplomats and merchants in Arcania.

### Ancestor Forges
Rather than burying their dead, Darkan return them to the earth through sacred forges. The ashes honor those who came before, and the forges continue their legacy of craftsmanship.

<Callout type="warning" title="The Hunger">
Every Darkan carries an echo of the Dark Titan's drive-a whisper that says *more, grow, consume*. Most manage this instinct easily. But some Darkan fall to the **Hunger**, becoming **Titan-Touched**: beings of immense power and zero restraint. Containing them is a sacred duty.
</Callout>

## The Path to Valoryn

From Redmire, the journey south to Valoryn is long but secure:

1. **The Mountain Descent** - Navigate treacherous passes with Darkan guides
2. **The Northern Road** - Well-patrolled route through allied territory
3. **The Crossing Fields** - Open plains requiring vigilance
4. **Valoryn's Mountain Gate** - Where Darkan sentinels never rest

The journey takes 4-5 weeks, during which travelers forge bonds as strong as steel.

## Notable Locations

### The First Forge
The oldest smithy in Redmire, said to be built where the first Darkan emerged from the earth. Still operational, producing ceremonial weapons for clan leaders.

### The Hall of Oaths
Where binding agreements are made. The walls are inscribed with every major oath in Darkan history-a record of promises kept across millennia.

### The Titan's Echo Chamber
A natural cavern that amplifies sound strangely. Darkan come here to face the whispers of the Dark Titan's hunger-and prove they are stronger than their origin.

### The Ancestral Vault
Repository of the greatest weapons ever forged by legendary Darkan smiths. Only in times of greatest need are these weapons drawn.
