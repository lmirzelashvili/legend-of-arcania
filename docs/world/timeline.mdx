### Key Events

**The Void Titan's Solitude**
For eons beyond counting, the Void Titan drifted alone-infinite emptiness given form. It knew no time, no change, no desire. Until curiosity stirred.

**Creation of the Dark Titan**
From its own essence, the Void Titan birthed a child: the Dark Titan. Where the father was stillness, the son was motion. Where the father was absence, the son was presence. A contradiction given form.

**The Anguish**
The Dark Titan's ambition knew no bounds. It grew until it rivaled its creator. When the Void Titan tried to contain it, they clashed-a battle that created the visible universe. Stars ignited from strikes. Black holes roared from wrath.

**Mutual Annihilation**
Neither Titan could destroy the other without destroying itself. Their final clash annihilated both, scattering their essences across creation. From this dramatic separation, Marael was born-the first conscious being.

### Key Events

**Marael's Purpose**
Born amid chaos, Marael brought order. He shaped the laws of physics, stabilized celestial bodies, gave structure to the universe. But purpose did not bring peace-he remained utterly alone.

**The Sleeping Light**
In the farthest reaches of the cosmos, Marael found something impossible: life. A world teeming with creatures surrounded a sleeping being of pure radiance. This was Anias, born from the Titans' regret, dreaming existence into being.

**The First World**
Before awakening, Anias's dreams had created the First World-primal forests, ancient beasts, life without intention. This unconscious creation would later become the source of Ranger power.

**Love's Beginning**
When Marael touched Anias, she awakened. Their meeting sparked the first love in existence-a bond that would create gods and eventually doom them.

### Key Events

**The Eterna Citadel**
Marael and Anias built a home above the world-a divine monument where they could observe creation. From here, they watched life evolve for millions of years.

**Divine Children**
Two children were born to the gods:
- **Erion** - Inheriting his father's strength and mother's compassion
- **Alassa** - Luminous and beautiful, drawn to mortal connection

**The Great Debate**
Marael watched mortals with suspicion, seeing their flaws. Anias watched with hope, seeing their potential. Their philosophical conflict would define the era-and eventually destroy it.

**Creation of Arcania**
Despite Marael's warnings, Anias convinced him to create **Arcania-The Land of Magic**: a blessed realm where gods and mortals could coexist. But Marael's agreement came with a decree: divine knowledge must never be shared.

### Key Events

**The Descent**
The gods took mortal forms and lived among their creations. For the first time, Erion and Alassa experienced true connection-friendship, love, the raw emotions that immortality had denied them.

**Erion and Nimue**
Erion fell in love with Nimue, a blacksmith's daughter who saw him as a man, not a god. Their love was pure-but Nimue's mortality haunted her.

**Alassa and Cassian**
Alassa loved Cassian, a charismatic general. When she discovered his betrayal with a servant, her rage was divine. She killed the servant's entire family and cursed Cassian to feel nothing-hollowing him.

**Consequences**
Anias, devastated by her daughter's violence, banished Alassa to the Eterna Citadel for a hundred years. In isolation, Alassa's compassionate heart froze. She began seeing mortals through her father's eyes.

### Key Events

**The Oracle's Poison**
The disgraced oracle Mildor preyed on Nimue's fear of mortality. He told her of a forbidden ritual: slay a goddess with a divine weapon, inherit her immortality.

**The Murder**
On the eve of her wedding, Nimue took Erion's sacred dagger-the Arcblade-and murdered Anias in her chambers. The goddess knew Nimue's intent but chose not to defend herself.

**The Spark of Light**
With her dying breath, Anias gave Erion the Spark of Light-the first light she ever created. She begged him to protect her dream: Arcania, and the mortals who lived there.

**The World Trembles**
When Anias died, her children and Marael felt it as if wounded themselves. Marael's grief became wrath. The sky split with storms. An age ended.

### Key Events

**The Dread Emissary**
Marael transformed Alassa into his weapon of vengeance. He gave her a new name-the Dread Emissary-and an artifact of terrible power: the Anguish Stone, forged from the essence of the Titans' clash.

**The Conquest**
With the Anguish Stone, Alassa commanded armies of nightmare creatures. She swept across Arcania, conquering territory at devastating speed. By Year 30, roughly 70% of the continent had fallen.

**The Resistance Forms**
Erion gathered survivors. Aldric became the First Paladin. The four mortal races-historically divided-united under shared threat. Valoryn was founded as the last bastion.

**Stalemate**
By Year 100, the initial conquest had stalled. Neither side could achieve decisive victory. The war settled into grinding attrition.

### Key Events

**The Grinding War**
For a century, the war continued without decisive change. Alassa held her Dominions. The Alliance held Valoryn. Countless soldiers died for miles of ground.

**The Vision**
When Marael attempted to end the war by speaking to Alassa, he was seized by a vision: the Dark Titan's will, reaching through the Anguish Stone, using Alassa to rebuild itself. The war had never been about vengeance-it was about cosmic resurrection.

**The Citadel Falls**
A colossal hand-fragment of the Dark Titan-emerged from a dimensional rift and crushed the Eterna Citadel. The divine home fell to earth, becoming the Throne of Ruin. Morgathis, the Hand itself, became a general.

**Marael's Choice**
Horrified by what he had enabled, Marael descended to the mortal realm and joined Erion. For the first time in two centuries, father and son fought together.

**The Current Day**
Year 200. With Marael's defection, the Alliance finally has hope of reaching the Throne of Ruin. The final campaign through the Five Dominions has begun. The war's end approaches-one way or another.
